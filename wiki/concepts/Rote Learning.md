# Concept: {{title}}

## 📖 Definition
A learning technique based on repetition and memorization of disjointed facts and algorithms, without deep understanding of the underlying principles or relationships.

## 📉 Impact on Transition
In school mathematics, rote learning is often successful for passing standard exams. However, it creates a significant barrier when students move to university, where they are expected to apply concepts flexibly and engage in **[[Concept/Advanced Mathematical Thinking]]**.
