## Definition & Core Idea
> 大一衝擊是指先前表現卓越的學生進入大學後，因課程難度激增與評量標準劇變而產生的突發性認知與情意混亂。

It differs from general stress because it specifically targets the student's **Mathematical Identity**. It involves the realization that high school talent is not enough, and the ensuing panic that they are "not who they thought they were."

## Key Dimensions / Components
- **Abruptness**: The shock occurs within the first 6-8 weeks.
- **Cognitive Dissonance**: Conflict between "I am good at math" and "I don't understand this lecture."
