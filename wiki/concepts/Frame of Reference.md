# Concept: {{title}}

## 📖 Definition
A psychological standard used by learners to evaluate their own abilities and achievements. 

## 🪜 Four Types in STT
Based on Geisler et al. (2023), students use four main frames during transition:
1. **Social (External)**: Comparison with peers.
2. **Dimensional (Internal)**: Comparison between different subject domains.
3. **Temporal**: Comparison with past performance (e.g., in high school).
4. **Criterial**: Comparison against fixed requirements (e.g., assignment points).
