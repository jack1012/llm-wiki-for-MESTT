## Definition & Core Idea
> Knowledge of procedures, knowledge of when and how to use them appropriately, and skill in performing them flexibly, accurately, and efficiently. Often contrasted with deeper conceptual understanding when misapplied as pure memorization.

## 📚 源出處 & 相關文獻 (Mentioned in Sources)

## ⚙️ Application in STT (Transition)
- While necessary, an over-reliance on procedural fluency in secondary school creates a false sense of mathematical mastery that shatters upon encountering university abstraction.
