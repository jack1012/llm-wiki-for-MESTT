## Definition & Core Idea
> 敘事認同 (Narrative Identity) 是指個體透過將過去、現在與想像的未來串聯成一個連貫的故事，從而獲得的自我一致感。

## Application in STT (Transition)
In transition research, students use narratives to justify their choices. A "Successful Transition Narrative" often involves the student reframing mathematical struggle as a positive challenge rather than an identity threat.
