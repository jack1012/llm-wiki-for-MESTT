## Definition & Core Idea
> 話語分析 (Discourse Analysis) 指的是研究語言使用如何與社會實踐、權力關係以及認同建構相互交織。

In STT research, it is used to examine how the language of school mathematics (which may be more supportive and personal) clashes with the language of university mathematics (which is often more abstract, formal, and impersonal). It looks at the "stories" students tell and how these stories position them as either "insiders" or "outsiders" in the mathematical world.

## Application in STT (Transition)
Hernandez-Martinez et al. (2008) use this to show that students' choice to continue with mathematics depends on whether they can adopt a mathematical discourse that fits their personal identity.
