# Concept: {{title}}

## 📖 Definition
A qualitative research methodology that focuses on collecting and analyzing stories or "narratives" people tell about their lives and experiences. It assumes that humans are "storytelling organisms."

## 🛠️ Application in Math Ed
Used to give voice to students who have struggled (Di Martino & Gregorio, 2019). Tools include semi-structured interviews, autobiographical writing, and open-ended questionnaires to capture the "authenticity, richness, and candor" of student experiences.
