## Definition & Core Idea
> 敘事研究是一種質性研究方法，它將「故事」視為理解人類經驗與社會現象的基礎。

In mathematics education research, narrative research (specifically through diaries, interviews, or autobiographies) allows researchers to access the **affective world** of students, capturing their emotions, shame, and subjective interpretations of failure that standard quantitative surveys often miss.

## Key Dimensions / Components
- **Subjectivity**: Focusing on the individual's "truth" rather than objective data.
- **Temporality**: Understanding how a crisis (like STT) develops over time.
- **Meaning-making**: How students reconcile their past success with current failure.

## Proponents & Origins

It is the primary tool for uncovering the "Hidden Crisis" during the first few months of university.
