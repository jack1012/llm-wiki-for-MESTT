## Definition & Core Idea
> 通融認知 (Commognition) 是由 Anna Sfard 提出的理論，認為思維是人類溝通的一種形式（對自己的溝通）。

It posits that learning mathematics is becoming a participant in a specific **mathematical discourse**, characterized by: (1) Word use, (2) Visual mediators, (3) Narratives, and (4) Routines. 

## Application in STT (Transition)
In STT research, the Commognitive Framework is used to analyze the conflict between school-mathematical discourse and university-mathematical discourse (e.g., how the word "function" or "proof" is used differently in each environment).
