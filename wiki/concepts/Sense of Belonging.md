## Definition & Core Idea
> 歸屬感是指學生感到自己被學術群體（數學系）接受、尊重並視為其成員的心理狀態。

A lack of sense of belonging is a primary driver for **Dropout** in STEM. For high-achievers, the loss of this sense occurs when they can no longer "keep up" with the discourse, leading to self-isolation and the feeling that they "don't belong in the world of professional mathematics."

## Key Dimensions / Components
- **Academic Integration**: Feeling competent within the academic rigors.
- **Social Integration**: Feeling connected to peers and professors.
- **Relatedness**: The basic human need to be connected to others in a shared pursuit.

## Application in STT (Transition)
Di Martino (2019) shows that students' sense of belonging is often "shattered" by **Criterial Feedback** that labels them as failures early on.
