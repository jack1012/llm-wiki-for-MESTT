# Concept: {{title}}

## 📖 Core Idea
Beliefs that knowledge is a set of established facts and rules that can be directly transmitted from teacher to student. In mathematics, this is closely associated with a **[[Concept/Static View of Mathematics]]** and surface learning approaches.
