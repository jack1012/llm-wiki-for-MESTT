## Definition & Core Idea
> 系統性文獻回顧 (SLR) 是一種利用詳盡且可重複的方法，針對特定研究問題進行定位、評估與合成所有現有高品質研究的方法論。

## Application in STT (Transition)
Crucial for synthesizing the fragmented landscape of STT research across different countries (e.g., German vs. Italian traditions).
