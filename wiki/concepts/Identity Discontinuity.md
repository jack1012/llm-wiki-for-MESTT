## Definition & Core Idea
> 認同不連續性是指學生在轉銜過程中，發現中學時期的「成功自我」與大學時期的「失敗自我」無法相容，進而產生心理斷層。

The "discontinuity" is not just in the mathematical content, but in the narrative the student tells themselves about who they are. When a student cannot find a bridge between these two versions of themselves, they experience **Identity Shock**.

## Key Dimensions / Components
- **Cognitive Conflict**: New abstract math vs. old computational skills.
- **Narrative Break**: A "top student" story suddenly becomes a "struggling student" story.
