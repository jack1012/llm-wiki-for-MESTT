## Definition & Core Idea
> 工具取向研究人類如何將一個「器物」(Artifact) 轉化為一個「工具」(Instrument)。這涉及了 **Instrumentation** (工具影響人) 與 **Instrumentalisation** (人決定工具用法) 的雙向過程。

## Application in STT (Transition)
Used to study how students navigate the change in digital and symbolic tools (e.g., CAS, Calculators, or LaTeX) as they move from secondary to tertiary levels.
