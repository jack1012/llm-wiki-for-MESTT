## Definition & Core Idea
> A set of practices and beliefs in mathematics education that value "able-bodiedness" and "able-mindedness" (characterized by speed, stoicism, and individualism) as the norm, while marginalizing students who deviate from this norm.

## 📚 源出處 & 相關文獻 (Mentioned in Sources)
- **[[summary-2024-監察院-教育部對大專學業退學生流向與制度公平性檢討調查報告]]**: 該研究系統性地分析了大學數學中的健全主義如何將學生形塑為「失能」的主體。

## ⚙️ Application in STT (Transition)
- In the secondary-to-tertiary transition, the rigid expectations for speed and individual performance in university math can be particularly disabling for students with learning differences, leading to a "shock start."
