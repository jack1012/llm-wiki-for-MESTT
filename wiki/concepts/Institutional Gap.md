## Definition & Core Idea
> 體制落差（Institutional Gap）是指雖然兩大體制（高中與大學）可能教授相同的題目（如極限、微分），但其背後的教學組織與認識論目標卻存在巨大鴻溝。

## Application in STT (Transition)
The gap is not typically in "content" but in "intent." While school focuses on utilitarian usage, university focuses on formal deductive structure.
