# Concept: {{title}}

## 📖 Definition
Within the **[[Concept/Person-environment-fit]]** framework, satisfactoriness refers to the viewpoint of the environment (university). It is an indicator of whether the student meets the external demands and requirements of the program.

## 📏 Measurement
Usually measured via objective achievement, such as exam scores or successful completion of weekly assignments.
