## Definition & Core Idea
> 認同建構 (Identity Construction) 是一個個體透過與他人互動、內化社會話語，並在實踐社群中定位自己的動態過程。

In the context of the **Secondary-Tertiary Transition (STT)**, it is the process through which a student renegotiates who they are in relation to mathematics. It involves the struggle between their "school-math identity" and the new, often alien, requirements of "university-math identity."

## Application in STT (Transition)
Understanding transition as identity construction explains why students often feel like "imposters" during their first year of university mathematics.
