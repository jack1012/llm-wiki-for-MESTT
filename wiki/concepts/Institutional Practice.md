## Definition & Core Idea
> 體制實踐（或體制實務）是指在特定社會或學術體制（如高中、大學、研究界）中，被視為「合法」與「標準」的行為與思考模式。

It suggests that what counts as "mathematical behavior" is defined by the institution.

## Application in STT (Transition)
Gueudet (2008) argues that students struggle during STT because they are forced to change their **Institutional Identity**. They move from an institution (school) that values procedural fluency to one (university) that values theoretical rigor and justification.
