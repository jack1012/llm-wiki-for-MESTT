# Concept: {{title}}

## 📖 Definition
Specific pedagogical or academic contexts (e.g., attending a lecture, working on a homework sheet, visiting a help desk) where students process content and experience affective shifts.

## 🔍 Key Situations in STT
- **Weekly Homework Tasks**: The primary site of competence testing.
- **Lectures**: Often the site of "abstraction shock."
- **Preparatory Courses**: Crucial for building early competence feelings.
