# Concept: {{title}}

## 📖 Core Idea
An emphasis on the technical performance of mathematics—using rules, techniques, and algorithms to "get an answer" without necessarily understanding the underlying theory or domain.

## ⚠️ Role in Transition Gap
High school mathematics assessment culture often prioritizes symbol manipulation. When students enter university, they attempt to apply this computational style to formal proofs, leading to failure when the "recipes" no longer apply.
