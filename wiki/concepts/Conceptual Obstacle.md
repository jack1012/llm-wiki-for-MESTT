# Concept: {{title}}

## 📖 Definition & Core Idea
> Internal constraints or difficulties within the learner's cognitive structure that make the transition to professional or formal knowledge difficult.

In the context of the secondary-tertiary transition, conceptual obstacles arise when students rely on algorithmic "shortcuts" that fail when faced with axiomatic rigor (Tall, 1991).
