## Definition & Core Idea
> 數學實務是指數學家或學生在特定社群（如數學系）中所從事的一系列社會性、體制性且受規則規範的活動（如證明、定義、猜想）。

It shifts the focus from "what" is taught (content) to "how" it is done (practice).

## Application in STT (Transition)
Gueudet et al. (2016) note a major trend in STT research: moving away from analyzing mathematical objects (e.g., limits) to analyzing the **Practice gap** between secondary and tertiary institutions.
