# Concept: {{title}}

## 📖 Definition
A positive affective stance towards mathematics that enables a student to persevere despite setbacks, frustrations, or an initial lack of intrinsic motivation.

## 🎓 Application in STT
Adapted from school contexts to university by Neumann et al. (2021). It is a key predictor of student persistence during the transition "shock."
