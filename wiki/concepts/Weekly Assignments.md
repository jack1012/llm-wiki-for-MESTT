# Concept: {{title}}

## 🧪 Description
Standard pedagogical practice in university mathematics (especially in Germany) requiring students to solve complex proof-based problems on a weekly basis.

## ⚠️ Significance in Transition
Research by Neumann et al. (2021) identifies weekly assignments as the most stressful and frustrating part of the first-year experience, as they shift students from the "front" to the "back" of mathematics.
