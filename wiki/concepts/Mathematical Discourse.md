## Definition & Core Idea
> 數學話語 (Mathematical Discourse) 指的是數學社群內傳達思想、建立論點及界定真理的特殊規則、符號系統與溝通模式。

## Application in STT (Transition)
Hernandez-Martinez (2008) highlights that university mathematics requires a leap into a "Formal/Symbolic Discourse" that is often disconnected from the "Narrative/Descriptive Discourse" used in school. The inability to master this discourse is a primary cause of **Identity Discontinuity**.
