## Definition & Core Idea
> 認同衝擊是指個體在環境劇烈變遷中（如 STT），原有建立在特定成就上的自我價值感發生崩解的現象。

In mathematics education, this occurs when a high-achieving student enters university and discovers that their previous "success formula" (e.g., rote learning, fast calculation) no longer works, leading them to question whether they are still "a math person."

## Key Dimensions / Components
- **Identity Discontinuity**: The gap between the "successful school-self" and the "struggling university-self."
- **Self-Worth Collapse**: When math performance is the sole pillar of self-esteem.
