# Concept: {{title}}

## 📖 Definition & Core Idea
> A state of mental disequilibrium that occurs when a student's prior knowledge or "naïve experience" is at variance with new, formal concepts.

Based on Piaget's work, it is a necessary stage for conceptual reconstruction. In the transition to university math, this occurs when high school algorithms clash with axiomatic definitions.
