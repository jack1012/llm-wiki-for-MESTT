# Concept: {{title}}

## 📖 Core Idea
A teaching and learning style that characterizes university mathematics, where concepts are introduced systematically on an axiomatic basis, requiring logical deduction and formal proof.

## 📉 Impact on Students
Many students encounter this approach for the first time at university, experiencing intense **[[Concept/Cognitive Conflict]]**. The jump from "informal/pre-formal" to "formal" understanding is a primary driver of the transition gap.
