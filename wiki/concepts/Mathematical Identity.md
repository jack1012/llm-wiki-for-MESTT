## Definition & Core Idea
> 數學認同是指一個人如何看待自己與數學的關係，以及他們如何在社會與學術語境中被認定為「數學能力者」。

It is a dynamic construct involving how students perceive their competence, their interest, and their belonging to the mathematical community. In many STT cases, mathematical identity is fragile because it is built on external grades rather than intrinsic understanding.

## Key Dimensions / Components
- **Competence**: Belief in one's ability to perform math tasks.
- **Recognition**: Being seen by others (teachers, peers) as good at math.
- **Interest**: Personal investment in the subject matter.
