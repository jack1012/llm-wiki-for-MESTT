## Definition
**Mathematical Organization (MO)** 是 [[Anthropological Theory of the Didactic]] (ATD) 中的核心組成部分。它將數學活動視為由四個要素組成的「實踐學」(Praxeology)：
1. **[T] Task (任務)**：要解決的問題型態。
2. **[τ] Technique (技術)**：解決任務的方法。
3. **[θ] Technology (技術解釋)**：解釋技術為何有效的理論基礎。
4. **[Θ] Theory (理論)**：支撐技術解釋的更深層理論架構。

## Role in STT (Transition)
In the transition from secondary to tertiary levels, the **MO** often undergoes a radical shift. High school mathematics focuses heavily on **Tasks** and **Techniques**, while university mathematics demands a rigorous understanding of **Technology** and **Theory**.
