# Concept: {{title}}

## 📖 Definition
The experience of unsuccessful academic outcomes (failing exams, repeating courses, or dropping out) within a mathematics degree program.

## 🔍 Characteristics in Transition
Research (Di Martino & Gregorio, 2019) focuses on the "First-time" nature of failure for high achievers. It is often unexpected and triggers intense negative emotions like shame and inadequacy, leading to a "mathematical malaise."
