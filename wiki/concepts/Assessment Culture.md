# Concept: {{title}}

## 📖 Core Idea
The prevailing norms and practices regarding how student performance is evaluated within an educational system, which significantly influences students' learning strategies.

## ⚠️ Impact in Transition
According to lecturers (Gruenwald et al., 2003), the assessment culture at school level often prioritizes passing exams over understanding. This leads to **[[Concept/Rote Learning]]** where knowledge is "stored in short-term memory" and lost after the final examination.
