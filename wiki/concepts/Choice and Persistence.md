## Definition & Core Idea
> 選擇與留存 (Choice and Persistence) 是指學生在面對學術危機（特別是 STT）時，決定繼續投入該領域而非轉向或退學的決策過程。

## Application in STT (Transition)
It is not a one-time event (like enrollment) but a continuous affective and cognitive process. Hernandez-Martinez (2008) argues that persistence is the result of successful **Identity Construction** within the university discourse.
