## Definition & Core Idea
> 人類學教學理論 (ATD) 是由 Yves Chevallard 提出的，旨在從體制（Institutional）的角度研究人類的數學活動。

The core construct is **Praxeology** (實踐學), which consists of a **Praxis block** (Task and Technique) and a **Logos block** (Technology and Theory).

## Application in STT (Transition)
ATD analyzes STT as a transition between different **Institutions** (School vs. University). It focuses on how mathematical tasks are performed and justified differently in university departments compared to high schools, highlighting the conflict in the "Logos" block.
