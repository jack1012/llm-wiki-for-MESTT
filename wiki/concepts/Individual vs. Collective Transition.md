## Definition & Core Idea
> 此概念對比了兩種解決 STT 的態度：將轉銜視為學生的個別責任（Individual），或是視為兩大體制（中學與大學）的共同責任（Collective）。

## Application in STT (Transition)
Gueudet (2008) argues that relying on individual adjustment is inefficient and leads to high dropout rates. A "Collective Transition" involves curriculum alignment and institutional dialogue between secondary and tertiary educators.
