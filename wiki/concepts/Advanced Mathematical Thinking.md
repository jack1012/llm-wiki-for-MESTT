# Concept: {{title}}

## 📖 Definition & Core Idea
> A mode of thinking that moves beyond simple computations into the realm of formal definitions, logical deduction, and axiomatic structures.

Advanced Mathematical Thinking (AMT) requires a "personal reconstruction" to build the cognitive apparatus necessary to handle concepts that are subtly at variance with intuition.

## 🏗️ Key Dimensions / Components
- **Mathematical Objects**: Moving from process to object.
- **Formal Definition**: The transition from a "Concept Image" to a strict "Concept Definition."
