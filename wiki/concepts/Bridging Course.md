# Concept: {{title}}

## 🧪 Purpose
Specific educational programs designed to fill the knowledge or thinking gap between secondary school and university mathematics.

## ⚠️ Challenges (from Literature)
- **Time Pressure**: Often too short (weeks) to change years of habit.
- **Cognitive level**: Often focus on remedial rules (Computational) rather than shifting students to higher-level (Formal) thinking.
