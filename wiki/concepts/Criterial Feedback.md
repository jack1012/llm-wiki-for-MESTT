# Concept: {{title}}

## 📖 Definition
Feedback provided to students based on fixed achievement criteria (e.g., scoring 5/10 on a proof assignment) rather than social comparison or personal improvement.

## ⚠️ Impact in Transition
Geisler et al. (2023) found that criterial feedback on weekly assignments is the dominant factor causing the decline of **[[Concept/Mathematical Self-concept]]** in university freshmen.
