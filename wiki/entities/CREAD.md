# CREAD
**Centre de Recherche sur l'Education, les Apprentissages et la Didactique**

## Overview
**CREAD** is a major educational research laboratory based in France, primarily associated with the **University of Rennes 2** and the **University of Western Brittany (UBO)**. It is a leading center for the study of didactics and learning processes.

## Role in STT Research
CREAD has been a central hub for developing multidimensional frameworks for the **[[Secondary-to-Tertiary Transition]]**. Researchers at CREAD, such as **[[Ghislaine Gueudet]]**, have utilized theories like the **[[Documentational Approach to Didactics (DAD)]]** and **[[Anthropological Theory of the Didactic (ATD)]]** to analyze the transition from institutional and social perspectives.
