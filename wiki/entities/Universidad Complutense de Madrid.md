# Universidad Complutense de Madrid (UCM)

## Overview
The **Universidad Complutense de Madrid** is one of the oldest and largest public research universities in Spain, located in Madrid.

## Role in STT Research
UCM was a major center for mathematics education research under the leadership of **[[Miguel de Guzmán]]**. It served as a primary research site for international comparative studies on the **[[Secondary-to-Tertiary Transition]] (STT)** during the 1990s.
