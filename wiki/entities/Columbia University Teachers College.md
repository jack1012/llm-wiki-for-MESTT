# Columbia University Teachers College

## Overview
**Teachers College (TC)** is the graduate school of education, health, and psychology at **Columbia University** in New York City. Founded in 1887, it is the oldest and largest graduate school of education in the United States.

## Role in Retention Research
TC has a long history of sociological research into educational attainment. **[[Vincent Tinto]]** conducted his foundational work on **[[Student Integration Theory]]** while affiliated with Teachers College (his 1973 review was published as an ERIC Report via the college).
