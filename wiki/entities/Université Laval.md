# Université Laval

## Overview
**Université Laval** is a French-language public research university in Quebec City, Canada.

## Role in STT Research
The university participated in the foundational 1998 international study on STT difficulties, representing the Canadian (specifically Quebecois) context. Research at Laval highlighted the significant differences in **[[Didactical Transposition]]** between the CEGEP system and university mathematics.
