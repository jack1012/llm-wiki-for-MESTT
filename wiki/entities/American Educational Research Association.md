# American Educational Research Association (AERA)

## Overview
The **American Educational Research Association (AERA)** is the largest national research society in the United States dedicated to the scientific study of education and learning.

## Role in STT Research
AERA publishes several key journals, including the **[[Review of Educational Research]]** and **[[Educational Researcher]]**, which have hosted foundational studies on student persistence, transition, and mathematics education. AERA’s annual meetings are a major venue for presenting new findings on the **[[Secondary-to-Tertiary Transition]]**.
