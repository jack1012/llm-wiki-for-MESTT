# Review of Educational Research (RER)

## Overview
The **Review of Educational Research (RER)** is a peer-reviewed academic journal published by the **[[American Educational Research Association]] (AERA)**. It is consistently ranked among the highest-impact journals in the field of education research.

## Role in STT/Retention Research
The journal was the platform for the publication of **[[Vincent Tinto]]'s** 1975 landmark paper, "**Dropout from Higher Education: A Theoretical Synthesis of Recent Research**." This publication brought the **[[Student Integration Theory]]** and the **[[Longitudinal Process Model]]** to a wide audience of education researchers and policymakers, establishing them as standard frameworks for the field.
