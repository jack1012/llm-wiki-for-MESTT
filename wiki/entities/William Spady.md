# William Spady

## Overview
**William Spady** is an American educational researcher and sociologist, often recognized as the "father" of **Outcome-Based Education (OBE)**.

## Role in Retention Research
Spady was the intellectual predecessor to **[[Vincent Tinto]]** in the study of college student dropout. In 1970, he was the first to explicitly apply **[[Émile Durkheim]]'s** sociological theory of suicide to the problem of college attrition. He argued that dropout could be understood through the lens of social and academic "normlessness" (anomie). Tinto’s 1973/1975 **[[Student Integration Theory]]** was a direct extension and refinement of Spady's sociological approach.
