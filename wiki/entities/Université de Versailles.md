# Université de Versailles Saint-Quentin-en-Yvelines

## Overview
**Université de Versailles** is a French public university founded in 1991. It is a major center for research in mathematics and science education.

## Role in STT Research
The university (specifically researchers like **Aline Robert**) participated in the foundational 1998 international study on STT difficulties. Research here focused on the **didactical layer** of transition, analyzing teacher practices and classroom interactions in first-year university mathematics.
