# U.S. Office of Education

## Overview
The **U.S. Office of Education** (now part of the U.S. Department of Education) was the federal agency responsible for education policy and research funding in the United States.

## Role in STT/Retention Research
In the late 1960s and early 1970s, the office funded major research syntheses on undergraduate retention to address growing concerns about student attrition in higher education. This funding supported **[[Vincent Tinto]]'s** development of the **[[Student Integration Theory]]** (1973), which eventually influenced national policy on student support services and first-year experience (FYE) programs.
