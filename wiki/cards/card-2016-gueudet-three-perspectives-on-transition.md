## Key Insight
Gueudet et al. classify STT research into three major lenses:
1. **Epistemological**: Focuses on the nature of mathematical knowledge and "epistemological obstacles" (Bachelard).
2. **Cognitive**: Focuses on individual learning processes, mental schemas (APOS), and conceptual change.
3. **Sociocultural**: Focuses on the transition between social groups, mathematical practices, and identities (Communities of Practice).
