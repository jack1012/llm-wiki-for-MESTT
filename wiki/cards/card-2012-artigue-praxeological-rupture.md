# Artigue 2012 - Praxeological Rupture

## The Concept
Using ATD, Artigue (2012) explains the transition gap as a **Praxeological Rupture**.

## The Mechanism
A **Praxeology** describes how we do math. It has two parts:
1.  **The Praxis (The Action)**: The tasks we solve and the techniques we use.
2.  **The Logos (The Thinking)**: The technology (explanations) and theory (definitions/proofs) that justify the action.

## The Rupture
- **In School**: The "Praxis" is huge, but the "Logos" is thin. Students learn how to calculate but the "why" is often left to intuition.
- **In University**: The "Logos" suddenly explodes. Techniques that were "obvious" now require rigorous proof.
- **The Gap**: Students try to solve university "Tasks" using school "Techniques" without grasping the new university "Logos."
