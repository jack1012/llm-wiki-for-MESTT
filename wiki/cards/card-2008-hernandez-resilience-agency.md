# Hernandez-Martinez 2008 - Resilience & Agency

## The Concept
Drawing on the Transmaths project, the researchers found that student **Agency** and **Resilience** are key mediators of successful STT.

## Key Findings
- **Resilience as Social Capital**: Students from disadvantaged backgrounds who succeed often demonstrate high resilience, using their social connections or personal determination to navigate institutional barriers.
- **Agency as Selection**: Successful students "actively choose" to belong to the mathematical community, rather than just "attending" classes.
- **Narrative of Success**: Resilient students frame setbacks as "challenges to be solved" (Internal, Unstable attribution) rather than "proof of inability."

## Significance
This highlights the importance of fostering a student's **Sense of Agency** during the transition period.
