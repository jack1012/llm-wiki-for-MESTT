## Key Insight
Vollstedt et al. propose six criteria to examine the transition shock via textbooks:
1. **Motivation** (Self-determination theory)
2. **Structure & Visual Representation**
3. **Concept Development** (Intro vs. Formal definition)
4. **Theorem Understanding**
5. **Proofs** (Existence of proof ideas)
6. **Tasks** (Level of cognitive demand)
Feasibility studies show a massive gap: school books provide "motivational additives" and "iconic visualization," while university materials are purely formal and lack didactical support for "how to prove."
