# Wasserman 2023 - Double Discontinuity Modern Take

## The Concept
Wasserman et al. (2023) revisit Felix Klein's **Double Discontinuity** (1908) which describes the two major shocks in a teacher's life.

## The Two Breaks
1.  **Transition 1 (School to University)**: The student enters university and finds that the mathematics is abstract and disconnected from what they learned in school.
2.  **Transition 2 (University to Teaching)**: The new teacher enters the classroom and finds that the advanced mathematics they learned at university seems to have no relevance to the secondary curriculum they must teach.

## Modern Insight
Wasserman argues that while the 1st discontinuity is a well-known "hurdle," the **2nd discontinuity** is the "utility crisis" that de-motivates teacher candidates throughout their degree. Solution: addressing the 2nd discontinuity *during* the degree helps solve the 1st discontinuity by providing meaning.
