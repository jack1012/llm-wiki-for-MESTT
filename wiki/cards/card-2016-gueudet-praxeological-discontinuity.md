# Gueudet 2016 - Praxeological Discontinuity

## The Concept
Drawing on **[[Marianna Bosch|Bosch (2016)]]** and the Anthropological Theory of the Didactic (ATD), this lens views the transition as a collision between two different **Praxeologies**.

## What is a Praxeology?
A praxeology (Task + Technique + Technology + Theory) defines how mathematics is practiced within an institution.
- **Secondary Praxeology**: Tasks (calculation) + Techniques (rules) + Technology/Theory (justification via examples).
- **Tertiary Praxeology**: Tasks (proof) + Techniques (logic) + Technology/Theory (Axioms and Theorems).

## The Discontinuity
The "Mathematical Crisis" occurs when the student attempts to apply a Secondary Techniqe to a Tertiary Task. The institution often fails to provide the bridge between the two, leaving the student unable to author a new, tertiary praxeology.
