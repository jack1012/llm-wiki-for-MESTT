## Key Insight
The transition to university math requires mastering the transformation from a **phenomenon-oriented view** (common in school) to a **formal language description** based on axioms and definitions. Research shows that school-acquired knowledge often provides an unreliable foundation for university-level notions like "limit" because the "habits of learning" (synonyms/descriptions vs. formal definitions) are in conflict.
