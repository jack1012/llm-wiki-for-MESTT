## Key Insight
Transition is conceptualized as the development of a **Professional Identity**. Through "Mathematical Moments," individuals transform from observers (children) to consumers (students), and finally to producers (researchers) or mediators (teachers) of mathematics. This process is influenced by role models and experiences of success, showing that STT is a life-long biographical project.
