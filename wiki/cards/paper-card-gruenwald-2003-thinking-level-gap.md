## 核心概念 (Core Concept: English Body)
> 一卡一概念：一張卡片只談一個概念，避免混雜。

- **Problem**: What is the root cause of the disconnect between school and university mathematics performance?
- **Method**: Cross-institutional survey comparing lecturer expectations with school curriculum.
- **Conclusion**: The core problem is a **Thinking Level Gap**. School mathematics focuses on "lower-order thinking skills" (LOTS) like memorization and algorithm application. University mathematics, however, demands "higher-order thinking skills" (HOTS), specifically [[Advanced Mathematical Thinking]]. 

The transition is failing because students are being asked to leap from "procedural mastery" to "conceptual abstraction" without a middle ground, leading to systemic performance collapse.

## 個人思考與關聯 (Reflection)
- **啟發與想法**: [[Thinking Level Gap]] 是最早被識別出的「結構性障礙」。
- **知識缺口**: 如何在高中三年級就引入部分高階思維訓練？
- **評論**: 這是經典研究，雖然是 2003 年，但其描述的「斷層」至今依然是全球數學教育者的痛點。
