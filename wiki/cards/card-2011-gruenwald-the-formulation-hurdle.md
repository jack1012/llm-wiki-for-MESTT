# Gruenwald 2011 - The Formulation Hurdle

## The Concept
Klymchuk & Gruenwald (2011) identify the **Formulation Step** as the most significant cognitive barrier for engineering and science students during transition.

## The Observation
- **Technical Competence**: Students can solve a given integral or derivative with 90% accuracy if the equation is provided.
- **Modelling Collapse**: When given a physical problem that *requires* setting up that same integral, success rates drop below 30%.
- **The Gap**: Secondary education focuses on "solving the ready-made equation," while Tertiary education expects students to "construct the equation from reality."

## Intervention
To reduce the gap, calculus courses must include "Modeling-from-scratch" tasks early on, even if the underlying algebra is simple, to build the mental bridge between context and symbol.
