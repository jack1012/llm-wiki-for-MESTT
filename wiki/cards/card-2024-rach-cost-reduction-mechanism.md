# Rach 2024 - Cost Reduction Mechanism

## The Concept
Rach (2024) identifies that **Profession-Related Tasks (PRTs)** not only increase value but also decrease the perceived **Costs** of learning.

## Dimensions of Cost
1.  **Emotional Cost**: The stress or anxiety associated with abstract math.
2.  **Effort Cost**: The feeling that the energy required is "not worth it."
3.  **Opportunity Cost**: Losing time for other activities.

## How PRTs Lower Costs
By providing a clear context (professional utility), the student's **cognitive appraisal** of the effort changes. The effort feels "meaningful" rather than "wasted on abstract nonsense," which reduces the psychological strain (Emotional Cost).

## Conclusion
PRTs act as a "buffer" against the emotional burnout common in the first semester of university mathematics.
