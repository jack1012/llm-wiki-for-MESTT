## Key Insight
Rezat proposes enhancing the classical **Didactic Triangle** (Student-Teacher-Mathematics) into a **Didactic Tetrahedron**. This model incorporates the **Textbook/Artifact** as a fourth element. In the context of STT, the transition is not just between people, but between different "Artifact Environments" (e.g., from a guided school textbook to autonomous university lecture notes).
