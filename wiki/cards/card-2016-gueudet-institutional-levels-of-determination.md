## Key Insight
Using Chevallard's Anthropological Theory of Didactics (ATD), the book illustrates how mathematical practices are shaped by "levels of determination": Civilisation -> Society -> School -> Pedagogy -> Discipline -> Domain -> Sector -> Theme -> Subject. Transition failures are often caused by hidden constraints at the higher (School/Society) levels.
