# Lovric 2011 - McMaster Intervention Triad

## The Practice
Miroslav Lovric (2011) describes the three-pronged strategy used at McMaster University to manage the "Rite of Passage."

## The Triad
1.  **Preparation (Mental & Content)**: The *Mathematics Review Manual*. Sent to students *before* they arrive, it sets the tone that "university math is different" and provides self-diagnostic tools.
2.  **Detection (Diagnostic Survey)**: Assessing not just content knowledge, but **Learning Attitudes**. This helps identifies students likely to struggle with the "liminal" stage.
3.  **Adjustment (Calculus Redesign)**: Slowing down the "pace" in critical first weeks to focus on **Logical Rigor** and **Proof-writing**, rather than rushing through content.

## Conclusion
Systemic intervention is more effective than "remedial" tutoring because it alters the institutional environment to accommodate the transition.
