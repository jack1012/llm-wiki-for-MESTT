## Key Insight
University "failure" (dropout/delay) is determined by four clusters: 1) Individual abilities and psychological traits; 2) Parental background and networks; 3) Institutional resources and costs; 4) Labor market returns. Mismatches in any cluster can trigger withdrawal.
