# Wasserman 2023 - Triple Connection Framework

## The Concept
Wasserman et al. (2023) categorize how advanced university mathematics (UM) relates to school mathematics (SM) into three types.

## The Framework
1.  **Superset**: UM covers SM as a special case, extending the scope (e.g., Complex numbers as a superset of Real numbers).
2.  **Subset-Concept**: UM provides the theoretical foundation for an SM concept (e.g., Abstract Algebra underlying the rules of school arithmetic).
3.  **Subset-Practice**: UM habits of mind (defining, proving, generalizing) that are directly applicable to SM pedagogical practices.

## Strategic Importance
Most interventions focus on *Subset-Concept*. Wasserman argues that *Subset-Practice* is potentially more powerful for making math "matter" to teachers, as it directly impacts their classroom actions.
