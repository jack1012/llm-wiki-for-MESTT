## 核心概念 (Core Concept: English Body)
> 一卡一概念：一張卡片只談一個概念，避免混雜。

- **Problem**: Does knowing how a student's interest *changes* add anything to our ability to predict dropout beyond just Knowing their entry-level scores?
- **Method**: Longitudinal regression (N=556) including entry-level affect (T1) and the change score (T2-T1).
- **Conclusion**: The **development (change)** of interest and self-concept significantly improves dropout prediction. Students who dropped out started with lower interest/self-concept, but crucially, they also experienced a significantly sharper *decline* during the first term compared to persisters.

This suggests that the "shock" of transition is not just a state, but a process of erosion. If interest in university mathematics and confidence in ability drop within the first 6-8 weeks, the risk of quitting before the second year increases dramatically. It's the "intra-individual change" that signals the breaking point.

## 個人思考與關聯 (Reflection)
- **啟發與想法**: 這對「早期預警系統」非常有啟發性。傳統預測只看入學考，但這篇證實了入學後前兩個月的「下滑速度」才是致死關鍵。
- **知識缺口**: 目前還需要釐清，這種下滑是源自於課程內容挫折，還是學習環境的社交孤立？
- **評論**: 極度認同。這提醒了大學講師，在學期中（而不是期末）就應該介入那些表現出興趣喪失的學生。
