## 核心概念 (Core Concept: English Body)
> 一卡一概念：一張卡片只談一個概念，避免混雜。

- **Problem**: Which type of mathematical belief leads to actual success in university mathematics?
- **Method**: Regression analysis of belief profiles and exam grades.
- **Conclusion**: There is a significant **Success Divergence** based on belief profiles. Students with **Dynamic View of Mathematics** (math as an evolving, discovered process) show higher persistence and better problem-solving flexibility. In contrast, those with a **Static View of Mathematics** (math as a fixed set of facts) are more vulnerable to burnout when their known rules no longer apply.

Interestingly, while the university system often *forces* students toward static beliefs (to survive exams), those who manage to *retain* their dynamic beliefs are the ones who ultimately excel and persist.

## 個人思考與關聯 (Reflection)
- **啟發與想法**: [[Dynamic View of Mathematics]] 是最好的心理防護服。
- **知識缺口**: 如何在量大且嚴苛的課程中，依然保留學生對數學探索的「動態感」？
- **評論**: 這揭示了制度（要求靜態）與卓越（需要動態）之間的矛盾。
