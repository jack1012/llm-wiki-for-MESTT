## Key Insight
Transformation is defined as the sum of scientific activities used to describe and understand the struggle for/with mathematics. In education, it encompasses the transformation of:
1. **Representations**: How signs and diagrams change meaning across contexts.
2. **Knowledge**: How scholarly math becomes school math (Didactic Transposition).
3. **Practice**: How solving strategies evolve into systematic algorithms.
4. **People**: How learners become teachers or researchers (Identity Transformation).
