# Artigue 2012 - The Institutional Nomad

## The Concept
Artigue (2012) uses the metaphor of the student as a **Nomad** traveling between different institutional "territories."

## The Journey
To succeed in STT, the student must:
1.  **Recognize the Border**: Understand that school math and university math are different territories with different laws.
2.  **Unpack the Baggage**: Let go of certain "school habits" (like seeking one fixed answer or waiting for the teacher's approval) that do not work in the new territory.
3.  **Adopt New Customs**: Learn the university "Logos" and the requirement for rigor and proof.

## Failure Case
A student remains a "Tourist" if they try to live by school rules while in the university. They eventually run out of resources and fail. The goal of transition programs is to help the nomad "settle" into the university culture.
