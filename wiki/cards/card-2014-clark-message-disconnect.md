# Clark 2014 - Message Disconnect

## The Concept
Clark & Lovric (2014) identify a major barrier to successful transition: the **Message Disconnect**.

## The Disconnect
- **Lecturers' Message**: "You are now responsible for your own learning. You must think rigorously and go beyond formulas."
- **Students' Perception**: "The teacher has stopped helping me. I am lost and I don't know how to do what they are asking because I've never been shown how."
- **Institutional Failure**: The university *expects* independence but doesn't *teach* how to be independent. The explicit message (Independence) lacks the necessary scaffolding (Guidance on how to be independent).

## Impact
This leads to a feeling of betrayal or abandonment among students, who then retreat into rote-learning habits as a survival mechanism, even though the lecturer explicitly told them not to.
