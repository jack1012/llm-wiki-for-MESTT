# Gueudet 2016 - Conceptual vs. Contextual Transitions

## The Concept
The ICME-13 survey (Gueudet, 2016) differentiates between two major dimensions of any educational transition.

## The Dimensions
1.  **Conceptual Transition (概念轉銜)**: Focuses on the learner's internal cognitive reorganization. It involves the shift from one mental model (e.g., arithmetic) to another (e.g., algebra).
2.  **Contextual Transition (情境轉銜)**: Focuses on the learner moving between social groups or institutions (e.g., high school vs. university department). It involves changes in **[[Didactical Contract]]**, authority, and social belonging.

## Significance in STT
A student might "understand" the concept (Conceptual success) but fail because they cannot adapt to the new social rules of the lecture hall (Contextual failure). STT requires addressing **both** dimensions simultaneously.
