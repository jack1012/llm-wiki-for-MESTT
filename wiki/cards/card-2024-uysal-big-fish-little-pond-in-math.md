## Key Insight
The "Big-Fish-Little-Pond Effect" (BFLPE) is a major affective hurdle during transition. High-achieving school students (the "big fish") enter university and suddenly feel like "small fish" among other experts, leading to a sudden and painful deflation of mathematical confidence.
