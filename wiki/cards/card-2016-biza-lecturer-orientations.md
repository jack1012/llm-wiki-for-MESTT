## Definition
Identifies two primary pedagogical orientations that university mathematics lecturers hold regarding the secondary-tertiary transition: the **Deficit-led** orientation and the **Pedagogy-led** orientation.

## Core Mechanisms
1.  **Deficit-led Orientation**:
    - View: Student failure is caused by "poor" secondary education and lack of procedural mastery.
    - Result: Focus on "remediating" student gaps through repetitive drilling or blaming previous levels.
2.  **Pedagogy-led Orientation**:
    - View: Student failure is caused by a "cognitive rupture" that requires new ways of thinking and being.
    - Result: Focus on explicitly teaching the transition as a new form of mathematical practice.

## Career Development Impact
> [!TIP]
> **職涯門檻效應**：如果學生遇到「缺憾型」講師，他們會將學業受挫內化為「能力不足」，進而產生「冒名頂替症候群」，阻礙其專業認同的形成。
