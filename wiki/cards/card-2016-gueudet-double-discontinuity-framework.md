## Key Insight
Building on Felix Klein (1908), the book defines the "Double Discontinuity" for pre-service teachers:
- **Discontinuity 1**: The shock of moving from school math to abstract university math.
- **Discontinuity 2**: The challenge of returning to school as a teacher and "transforming" university knowledge back into elementary content for teaching.
