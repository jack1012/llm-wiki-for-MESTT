# Winslow 2009 - Complementarity of Frameworks

## The Concept
The GOA-model helps researchers achieve **Complementarity** when analyzing complex STT phenomena.

## Practical Application
When studying university transition, a researcher might:
1.  Use **ATD (O-focus)** to map the disconnect between school and university **[[Praxeology (ATD)|praxeologies]]**.
2.  Use **Constructivism/Phenomenography (G-focus)** to understand the student's **[[Mathematical Self-concept|self-concept]]** during the transition.
3.  Use **Discourse Theory (A-focus)** to analyze the shift in **[[Discursive Framework for Transitions|mathematical language]]**.

## Summary
The GOA-model provides the "map" that allows these diverse tools to work together without theoretical contradiction, by identifying which part of the "Epistemic System" each one is addressing.
