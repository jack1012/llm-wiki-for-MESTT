# Gueudet 2016 - Deficit to Opportunity Shift

## The Concept
A major theme of the ICME-13 survey was the need to shift from a **Deficit Model** to an **Opportunities for Learning Model**.

## The Shift
- **Deficit Model**: Asks "What is wrong with the student?" or "What knowledge gap is causing the failure?" Focus is on remedial fixing of prerequisites.
- **Opportunity Model**: Asks "How does this transition challenge provide a unique site for the student to develop high-level mathematical maturity?" Transition is seen as a productive struggle that forces the reconstruction of knowledge.

## Why it matters
The deficit model often leads to "bridging courses" that merely repeat school content, while the opportunity model leads to "induction courses" that engage students in authentic mathematical discourse.
