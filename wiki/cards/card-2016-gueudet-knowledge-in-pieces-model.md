## Key Insight
DiSessa (Chapter 2) argues for a "continuist" view of transition using the "Knowledge in Pieces" (KiP) model. Instead of seeing transition as a sudden "break," it is viewed as a process of weaving "naive strands" of thought into a new, more systematic scientific fabric.
