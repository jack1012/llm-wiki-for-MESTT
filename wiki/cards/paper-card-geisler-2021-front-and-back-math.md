## 核心概念 (Core Concept: English Body)
> 一卡一概念：一張卡片只談一個概念，避免混雜。

- **Problem**: How does the way mathematics is presented (Front vs. Back) affect students' belief adjustment during the transition?
- **Method**: Theoretical framework applied to student interview data.
- **Conclusion**: University mathematics focuses heavily on the **"Back of Mathematics"** (formal definitions, logic, proofs, and the finished product), whereas secondary school mathematics often stays in the **"Front of Mathematics"** (mathematical exploration, context, and intuition). 

This mismatch causes students to feel that they are "no longer doing the same subject." The adjustment of beliefs from dynamic/process-oriented to static/formal-oriented often results in a loss of interest, as students struggle to find the "creative" part of math in the university's formal structures.

## 個人思考與關聯 (Reflection)
- **啟發與想法**: [[Front and Back of Mathematics]] 是一個非常有力的對比。我們是否可以在大學課堂中揭示更多「前台」的發現過程，而不只是展示「後台」的完美產品？
- **知識缺口**: 目前較少研究討論如何設計「橋接前與後的教學活動」。
- **評論**: 這解釋了 [[Static View of Mathematics]] 為何會在進入大學後佔據主導地位——因為課程只給你看後台。
