# Rach 2024 - Utility Value Boost PRTs

## The Concept
Rach (2024) shows that **Profession-Related Tasks (PRTs)** significantly boost students' perceived **Utility Value** of university mathematics.

## Mechanism
- **Direct Link**: PRTs show pre-service teachers exactly how an abstract university concept (e.g., group theory) underpins a school concept (e.g., number systems).
- **Motivation Restoration**: By seeing the "utility" for their future career, students who were alienated by abstraction regain a sense of purpose.
- **Intrinsic Spillage**: Interestingly, boosting utility value also tended to increase **Intrinsic Value** (enjoyment of the math itself).

## Importance in STT
For teacher education students, the STT is often a "career-identity crisis." PRTs allow them to keep their "teacher" identity while engaging with "mathematician" content.
