# Hernandez-Martinez 2008 - Transition as Identity Opportunity

## The Concept
Hernandez-Martinez (2008) argues against the "Trouble" narrative of transitions, suggesting instead that students see it as an **Identity Construction Opportunity**.

## Key Insights
- **Beyond Deficit**: Instead of asking "what did school fail to teach?", we should ask "who is the student becoming?".
- **Productive Struggle**: Many students reported that the "difficulty" of university math was exactly what made it feel meaningful and "different" from school.
- **Narrative Reconstruction**: Success in STT involves the student rewriting their life story from "I was good at school math" to "I am becoming a mathematical professional."

## Pedagogical Implication
Institutions should provide environments that support **Identity Work**, not just remedial content.
