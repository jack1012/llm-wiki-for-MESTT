## Key Insight
Finnish learning environments (e.g., Extreme Apprenticeship) provide high levels of "institutional scaffolding." This support allows even students with performance goals to find coping mechanisms, making mathematical tasks feel more "conducive" to their needs compared to traditional German settings.
