## Key Insight
Pepin views transition as a transformation of people, tools, and practices. A key driver is **Feedback**. She identifies four levels of feedback: task, process, self-monitoring, and personal evaluation. At university, students must move from "other-regulated" feedback (teacher-led) to "self-regulated" learning, where the lecture acts as a "catalyst" for independent study rather than a direct transmission of knowledge.
