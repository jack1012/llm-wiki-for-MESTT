## 核心概念 (Core Concept: English Body)
> 一卡一概念：一張卡片只談一個概念，避免混雜。

- **Problem**: Does the transition from high school to university necessarily lead to more "sophisticated" mathematical beliefs?
- **Method**: Longitudinal study tracking belief changes over the first year.
- **Conclusion**: Instead of becoming more sophisticated, many students undergo a **"Dysfunctional Belief Adjustment"**. They move from a discovery-oriented belief system to a rote-oriented, static one as a survival strategy to pass exams. 

This suggests that the environmental pressure of university mathematics can actually "regress" students' mathematical philosophy, forcing them to abandon [[Constructivist Beliefs]] in favor of [[Transmissive Beliefs]] to cope with the workload.

## 個人思考與關聯 (Reflection)
- **啟發與想法**: 這是非常令人遺憾的發現。大學教育原本應該開啟思維，結果卻可能逼迫學生回歸死記硬背。
- **知識缺口**: 我們如何避免考試制度扼殺學生的動態數學信念？
- **評論**: [[Static View of Mathematics]] 在這篇研究中被證明是環境壓力下的產物，而非自然的成長。
