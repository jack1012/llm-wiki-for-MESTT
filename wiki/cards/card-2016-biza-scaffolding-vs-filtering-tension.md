# Biza 2016 - Scaffolding vs. Filtering Tension

## The Concept
University math lecturers experience an internal role conflict known as the **Scaffolding vs. Filtering** tension.

## The Tension
- **Scaffolding (The Support Role)**: The desire to provide pedagogical aids (notes, tutorials, simpler examples) to help students survive the transition.
- **Filtering (The Guard Role)**: The institutional belief that mathematics is a "gatekeeper" and that lowering standards to help students pass "dilutes" the degree and passes on under-prepared students to higher levels.

## Impact on STT
This tension often results in inconsistent "didactic contracts." A lecturer might provide a lot of "scaffolding" in lectures, but then set a "filtering" exam that requires far more abstract rigor, leading to high failure rates and student disillusionment.
