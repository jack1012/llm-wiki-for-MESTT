# Lovric 2011 - Culture Shock Dimensions

## The Concept
Lovric (2011) argues that the transition to university math is a form of **Culture Shock**.

## The Dimensions
- **Language**: Symbols in university (e.g., $\forall, \exists$) are not just "shorthand" but are used in rigorous, logical arguments that students struggle to "read."
- **Rigor**: The shift from "it works in these examples" to "provided the following axioms hold."
- **Social Structure**: The shift from being a "star student" with a close relationship with a teacher to being an "anonymous student" responsible for their own success.
- **Pace**: The massive increase in the volume of new definitions delivered per week.

## Impact
This shock can lead to temporary "Cognitive Paralysis," where even talented students stop performing until they adapt to the new "culture."
