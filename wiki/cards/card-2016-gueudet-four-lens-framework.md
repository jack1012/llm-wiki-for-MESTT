# Gueudet 2016 - Four-Lens Framework

## The Concept
Gueudet et al. (2016) categorize the vast literature on transitions into four distinct analytical lenses.

## The Lenses
1.  **Epistemological**: Investigates the nature of mathematical knowledge and the shift from tools to objects of study.
2.  **Cognitive**: Focuses on individual mental processes, concept images, and specific learning obstacles.
3.  **Institutional**: Examines the role of "Didactical Contracts," institutional praxeologies, and administrative structures.
4.  **Sociocultural**: Looks at identity, belonging, and the social contexts of students (e.g., gender, socio-economic status).

## Significance
This framework allows researchers to locate their work within a broader map and identifies that most "crises" are multifaceted, involving at least two or three of these lenses simultaneously.
