# Klymchuk 2014 - Independence Expectation Gap

## The Concept
A major finding in Klymchuk et al. (2014) is the massive "mismatch" in assumptions about **Student Independence**.

## The Gap
- **Secondary Teachers**: Believe it is their job to "walk the student through" every detail to ensure success. They provide scaffolded notes and constant reminders.
- **Tertiary Lecturers**: Assume that by age 18, a student is an **Autonomous Learner**. They provide the "big picture" and expect students to fill in the proofs and exercises themselves.

## Conclusion
The "unbalanced expectations" cause students to feel abandoned (Lacking **[[Personal-Environment Fit]]**). The study suggests that the final year of high school should intentionally build "independence-for-university," rather than just "content-for-exams."
