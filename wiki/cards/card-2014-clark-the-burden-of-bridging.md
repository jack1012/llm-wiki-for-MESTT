# Clark 2014 - The Burden of Bridging

## The Concept
Clark (2014) notes that the institutional "responsibility" for bridging the transition is often unfairly placed entirely on the student.

## The Observation
- **Instructional Silence**: Lecturers often assume that if they provide resources (books, websites), they have "bridged the gap."
- **Student Burden**: The student is expected to recognize their own gaps, find the resources, and catch up while also attending 20 hours of new lectures.
- **Cognitive Exhaustion**: Placing the "Burden of Bridging" solely on the newcomer often leads to burnout and a sense that they are "not good enough" to be there.

## Conclusion
A "Double-sided Bridge" is required: students must put in effort, but the university must provide **Time** and **Structural Scaffolding** specifically for the transition, recognizing it as a course in itself.
