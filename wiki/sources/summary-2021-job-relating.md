## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P2：制度碰撞問題
- **Research Problem**: How does the praxeological framework reveal the institutional constraints and misalignments between school and university mathematics curricula in the STT?
- **Key Results**: Praxeological analysis reveals fundamental misalignments between school and university curricula; Belgian mathematics education shows distinct institutional constraints that impede transition; explicit praxeological awareness supports curricular redesign and student adaptation.
- **Methodology**: Mixed-methods praxeological study; curriculum analysis using ATD framework; case analysis of Belgian secondary-tertiary mathematics systems.
