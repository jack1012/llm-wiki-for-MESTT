## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P5：理論碎片化問題
- **Research Problem**: How do we systematically define and synthesize the state-of-the-art developments in core domains of tertiary mathematics education, affect, and socio-cultural paradigms?
- **Key Results**: The encyclopedia entries provide definitive overviews. The STT entry (Gueudet & Thomas) synthesizes institutional culture differences, proof practice gaps, and bridging solutions. The Attitude entry (Zan & Di Martino) outlines the TMA model (Emotions, Vision of Mathematics, Perceived Competence). The Socioepistemology entry (Cantoral) shifts the focus from object-based cognition to "knowledge in use" within nested social practices.
- **Methodology**: Extensive literature reviews and theoretical syntheses by leading field experts.
