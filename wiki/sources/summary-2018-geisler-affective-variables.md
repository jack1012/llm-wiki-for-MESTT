## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: High dropout rates match sharp declines in affect, but the specific learning situations triggering this decline are rarely identified for intervention.
- **Research Problem**: To track the longitudinal development of interest and self-concept during the first semester and identify specific situations leading to affective decline.
- **Key Results**: Dropout students show significantly steeper declines in interest and self-concept. Critical situations identified include homework tasks and lack of feedback, where self-efficacy is most challenged.
- **Methodology**: Mixed-methods longitudinal study: Quantitative (N=556) + Qualitative interviews (N=10).
