## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Undergraduate tutors are a key but under-researched part of the STT support system; their teaching is heavily influenced by their own recent transition experiences.
- **Research Problem**: To explore how tutors use their personal narratives of transitioning to university mathematics to interpret and act on teaching incidents.
- **Key Results**: Finds that tutors often use "empathy" based on their own past struggles to guide students, but they may inadvertently replicate procedural approaches they used to survive the transition.
- **Methodology**: Narrative analysis of semi-structured interviews and reflective journals from 15 tutors.
