## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Learning obstacles in the secondary-tertiary transition are well-documented but lack systematic categorization using didactical frameworks, limiting pedagogical design and teacher preparation.
- **Research Problem**: To systematically review and categorize students' obstacles and learning barriers in the secondary-to-tertiary transition in mathematics, using Didactical Design Research (DDR) frameworks to organize findings and identify intervention points.
- **Key Results**: ## 關鍵發見 (Key Findings)
- **Methodology**: Systematic literature review using DDR framework; thematic analysis of obstacles across cognitive, epistemological, and institutional dimensions; Indonesian and international research bases.
