## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Graduate student dropout rates in Taiwan (Day Master's 14%, Professional Master's 16%, PhD 21%) are substantial but poorly understood at the institutional level. Existing data is disaggregated but not analytically clustered.
- **Research Problem**: What are the distinct institutional patterns of master's degree student dropout across Taiwan's 70 general universities, and can cluster analysis reveal actionable typologies?
- **Key Results**:
  1. **Work Demand Dominates** (32.94%): The single largest reason for master's student dropout is work-related conflict — students leave to meet employment demands, not because they failed academically.
  2. **Three Institutional Clusters Identified**:
     - **Cluster 1 (Low-Attrition / Exit-Driven)**: Universities where "Military Service" and "Going Abroad" are relatively more common — students leave for **planned transitions**, not crisis dropout.
     - **Cluster 2 (High Work-Demand Schools)**: Universities where work-related dropout dominates — typically schools serving working professionals or part-time graduate students.
     - **Cluster 3 (Academic-Interest Mismatch)**: Universities where "Academic/Thesis Difficulty" (17.07%) and "Interest Mismatch" are elevated — suggests inadequate program-student fit at admission.
  3. **PhD Attrition Highest** (21%): Consistent with international findings that doctoral programs have higher non-completion due to longer time-to-degree and greater thesis-dependency.
  4. **Overall Average**: Across all clusters, average dropout proportion ≈ 20.58%.
- **Methodology**: K-means cluster analysis (k=3) using 107 Academic Year data from the MOE IR platform. Consolidated 15 original dropout reason categories into 9 analytical clusters.

## Structured Reading Notes (中文摘要)
- **研究主題**: 以校務研究方法分析台灣70所一般大學碩士班休學原因之機構差異。
- **核心發現**:
    - **工作需求主導**: 32.94% 的碩士生因工作需求休學，超越任何學術因素。
    - **機構分群**: 三類學校集群呈現截然不同的休學模式，說明「退場原因」具有學校類型特異性，非全台一致。
    - **博士生風險最高**: 博士班 21% 的休學率反映了「論文瓶頸」與「長學制壓力」的特殊挑戰。
- **貢獻**: 率先以 IR 平台進行碩士班機構集群分析，填補了碩士階段退場研究的空缺。

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：林靜慧等 (2021a) 揭示了 **「職涯-學業衝突 (Career-Study Conflict)」** 是台灣研究所退場的主導機制，而非學術失敗。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：碩士班學生大量因「工作需求」休學，映射出台灣職場對學歷的矛盾態度：雇主要求學歷提升，但不同時提供足夠的時間或經濟支援讓員工完成學業。這是一種 **「制度性雙重束縛 (Institutional Double Bind)」** — 勞動市場要求碩士學位，卻同時透過工作負荷阻礙其取得。
*   **職涯多樣性視角**: 三種集群代表了截然不同的職涯-學業互動模式：計畫性轉銜（群1）、職業優先者（群2）、學術-興趣失配者（群3）。
*   **重構方向**: 應將「休學」重新框架為 **「職涯階段重組」** 而非「學業失敗」，並設計更靈活的研究所修業制度以容納職涯驅動的學習節奏。
