## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: The global trend toward academic flexibility (abolishing dismissal policies, removing credit minimums) aims to encourage exploration and cross-disciplinary learning. However, its unintended consequences on at-risk students' persistence and quality are rarely examined in elite institutions.
- **Research Problem**: How do policy changes in NCTU impact students' behaviors in double majors/minors, course withdrawals, and academic failure rates?
- **Key Results**: (1) **The Price of Flexibility**: After abolishing dismissal, students with previous failure records saw their failure rates increase by another **3.54%**, suggesting that the removal of external threats leads to "Academic Drift" for at-risk groups. (2) **Strategic Withdrawal**: Removing the 15-credit minimum led to a surge in course withdrawals (停修), which students use to "beautify" their GPA at the cost of late graduation. (3) **The Late Graduation Paradox**: While double majoring/minoring has become more popular, it significantly extends the time to professional entry. (4) **Demographic Hazard**: Male students and those admitted via specific alternative channels exhibit significantly higher failure rates (**7.5% higher** than females), indicating a gendered dimension in transition adjustment.
- **Methodology**: Random-effects models and panel data analysis (104-111 academic years).

## Structured Reading Notes (中文摘要)
- **研究主題**: 陽明交大校務制度彈性化對學生學業行為之衝擊分析。
- **核心發現**:
    - **學術自由的副作用**: 廢除退學制後，原本處於及格邊緣的學生因失去強制性約束，學業表現進一步下滑，形成了「溫柔的放棄」。
    - **GPA 的美容術**: 停修與退學制度的廢除共同構成了一種避險機制，讓學生在行政上維持「存活」，但實質專業能力累積出現斷層。
    - **跨域的延遲效應**: 雙主修與輔系的普及雖然提升了跨域能力，但伴隨而來的延畢現象推遲了學生的職涯起點。
- **貢獻**: 定義了頂大轉銜中的「隱性中輟」現象，並呼籲建立更精準的預警機制。

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：陳致均 (2023) 揭示了轉銜策略中的 **「學術自由的幻覺：彈性制度下的專業停滯 (The Illusion of Academic Freedom: Professional Stagnation in Flexible Systems)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：陳致均的研究揭露了頂大轉銜中一個極其隱晦的威脅：**「隱性中輟 (Invisible Dropouts)」**。當大學系統廢除了退學制度，對於適應不良的學生而言，這不僅是壓力釋放，更可能是一種「慢性職涯自殺」。他們在行政上仍是「學生」，但在專業成長上已陷入停滯。這種「學術自由的幻覺」讓他們在不適合的校系中反覆掙扎，產生了巨大的 **「職涯機會成本 (Career Opportunity Cost)」**。這提醒我們：轉銜支持應是 **「主動導航」** 而非 **「行政放任」**。對精英學生的真正關懷，應是協助他們在自由中找到專業定錨，而非看著他們在自由中迷失。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未深入分析「輔系/雙主修」的成功者與失敗者在職涯定向感上的差異。
*   **重構方向**：應將「學分獲取效率」標註為衡量 **「專業成長動能」** 的核心指標。
