## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: There is a significant mismatch between student expectations and the actual rigor required in college-level STEM, leading to high attrition even among students who took science in high school.
- **Research Problem**: Which specific high school mathematics and science courses are the strongest predictors of success in college-level STEM courses?
- **Key Results**: Identifies **Higher-level Mathematics** (Trigonometry and Calculus) as the most powerful and consistent predictors of college success across *all* STEM disciplines. Specifically, high school **Trigonometry** is the critical threshold for College Algebra success, while **Calculus** serves as a proxy for the general logical rigor required even in non-math subjects like Biology. The study highlights a **"Rigor Gap"**: standard high school science courses often lack the mathematical depth required to bridge students into college-level inquiry.
- **Methodology**: Quantitative longitudinal analysis of course-taking patterns and GPAs in a large US state university system.

    - High school Trigonometry is often the "hidden bottleneck" that causes failure in early college STEM.
    - The transition is a **"Rigor Shock"** for students who rely on intuitive science without a mathematical foundation.
- **Contribution**: Quantified the "Math-First" strategy for STEM retention.

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：Culpeper (2010) 揭示了高中選課作為 **「職涯路徑的結構性預測指標 (Structural Predictors of Career Stability)」** 的角色。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：轉銜的成敗往往在國三至高二的選課決策中就已決定。Culpeper 建議的「早期識別」本質上是 **「職涯導航的校準」**。如果學生在職涯規劃中設定了 STEM 目標，但未能選修高階三角學或微積分，他實際上是在進行一場 **「高風險的職涯博弈」**。高階數學課程不僅是學分，更是 **「職涯韌性的訓練營」**。選修 Calculus 本質上是學生對未來職業嚴謹性的一種 **「預先承諾 (Pre-commitment)」**。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未討論選課建議權限（如輔導老師、家長）如何影響學生的這項職涯投資決定。
*   **重構方向**：應將「高階數學選修率」標註為衡量 **「職涯導航精確度」** 的核心指標。
