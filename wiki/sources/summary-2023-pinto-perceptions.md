## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P6：教師盲區問題
- **Research Problem**: What are the diverse perspectives, experiences, and proposed interventions held by university mathematics instructors globally regarding the secondary-tertiary transition, and how do these perspectives shape institutional responses to STT challenges?
- **Key Results**: International survey reveals a typology of teacher perspectives ranging from deficit-focused (blaming school preparation) to systemic-focused (institutional change); teachers acknowledge STT challenges but differ in whether they view problems as student/school deficits or university curriculum issues; perception gaps impede coherent institutional responses.
- **Methodology**: Global survey of university mathematics instructors (N=300+); international mixed-methods study; narrative analysis of teacher perspectives; comparative case analysis across institutional contexts.
