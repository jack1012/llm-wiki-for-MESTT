## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Transition research has historically been cognitive-focused, but there is a need for "new insights" that combine cognitive, affective, and sociocultural dimensions within a unified framework.
- **Research Problem**: To present a synthesized overview of recent STT research trends, emphasizing the networking of theories and the role of identity.
- **Key Results**: Highlights the emergence of "Theory Networking" and the concept of transition as a sociocultural "Rite of Passage". Emphasizes the need for institutional changes, not just student support.
- **Methodology**: Systematic review and expert synthesis.
