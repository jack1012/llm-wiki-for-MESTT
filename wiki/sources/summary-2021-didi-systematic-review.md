## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Despite 40 years of research, STT dropout rates remain high, suggesting that early cognitive-only models were insufficient.
- **Research Problem**: To conduct a systematic review of STT publications from the last 13 years (2008–2021) to identify emerging trends and gaps.
- **Key Results**: Finds a significant increase in research on "Affect" (interest, identity) and "Networking of Theories". Points out that "Intervention Effectiveness" is still the least researched area.
- **Methodology**: Systematic review of 120+ peer-reviewed articles.
