## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Universities often implement support systems based on *lecturer* perceptions of student needs, which may differ from students' *actual* lived experiences.
- **Research Problem**: To capture a high-resolution "snapshot" of student perceptions regarding three facets of transition: Academic, Social, and Institutional.
- **Key Results**: Students perceive the "Academic Gap" (content speed/abstraction) as the primary stressor, but the "Social Gap" (isolation in large lectures) is what most impacts their resilience.
- **Methodology**: Large-scale survey; N=400+; qualitative thematic analysis.
