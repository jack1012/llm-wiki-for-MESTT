## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: STT research has grown exponentially but remains fragmented across different theoretical frameworks, making it difficult for practitioners to extract unified guidelines.
- **Research Problem**: To conduct a systematic review of STT literature from the last 20 years, focusing on theoretical trends and the "affective turn".
- **Key Results**: Identifies a shift from cognitive analysis to sociocultural and affective perspectives. Points out that "Identity" and "Beliefs" are now central to understanding student failure.
- **Methodology**: Systematic review of 150+ peer-reviewed articles.
