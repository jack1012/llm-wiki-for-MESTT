## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Universities often assume a "common core" of prerequisites, but the actual impact of specific topic-related prior knowledge on tertiary success is not well understood.
- **Research Problem**: To analyze the relationship between school-level mathematical prerequisites (calculus, linear algebra) and performance in the first semester of university.
- **Key Results**: Found that procedural mastery of calculus is a strong predictor of early success, but conceptual understanding of "Proof" is the actual bottleneck for long-term retention.
- **Methodology**: Quantitative analysis of diagnostic test data and semester outcomes; N=800+.
