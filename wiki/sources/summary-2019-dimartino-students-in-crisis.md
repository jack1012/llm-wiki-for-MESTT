## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P3：情意-身分危機問題
- **Research Problem**: Investigating the **Affective Factors** and **Cognitive Conflicts** that trigger **Identity Shock** when successful students encounter the formal rigor of university mathematics.
- **Key Results**: The transition crisis manifests as a collapse of **Mathematical Identity**. Using **Narrative Analysis**, the study finds that students attribute failure to internal, stable factors, leading to a "Crisis of Self" that precedes formal academic failure.
- **Methodology**: Qualitative narrative analysis; semi-structured interviews with first-year university students; critical incident analysis; examination of mathematical identity and affective trajectories during crisis.
