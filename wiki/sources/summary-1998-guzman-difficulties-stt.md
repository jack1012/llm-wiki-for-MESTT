## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: The global STT "stumbling block" is caused by a systemic mismatch where university teachers' "ideal student" expectations conflict with students' algorithmic toolkits.
- **Research Problem**: To categorize the multifaceted sources of student failure in university mathematics across different national contexts (Canada, France, Italy, Spain).
- **Key Results**: Established a three-pillar taxonomy: (I) Epistemological (nature of math thinking); (II) Sociological (institutional size/climate); (III) Didactical (pedagogical contract mismatch).
- **Methodology**: Synthesis of international national reports and student surveys across multiple science and engineering faculties.
