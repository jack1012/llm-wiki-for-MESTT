## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: The shift in secondary education toward "modular assessment" and "accessibility" has created a **False Signal of Readiness**. Students achieve high grades but lack the underlying fluency required for the rigors of university STEM.
- **Research Problem**: How have curriculum changes in the UK (e.g., A-levels reform) affected the algebraic preparedness and reasoning skills of entering university students?
- **Key Results**: Identifies a systemic **"Structural Gap"**. While students are proficient in numerical and graphical checking (often mediated by calculators), they fail at **Symbolic Manipulation and Proof**. The study highlights a **"Double Discontinuity"**: (1) The gap between school math and university math; (2) The gap between university math and professional application. It argues that "accessibility" in school has come at the cost of "structural depth," leaving students with a fragile foundation for calculus and beyond.
- **Methodology**: Quantitative analysis of student performance data and longitudinal diagnostic testing across UK higher education institutions.

## Structured Reading Notes
- **Theoretical Framework**: Klein’s Double Discontinuity; Curriculum Backwash Effect.
- **Core Concepts**: [[Structural Algebraic Competence]], [[Procedural Fluency vs. Structural Insight]], [[Modular Fragmentation]], [[The Proof Deficit]].
- **Main Findings**: 
    - Grade A students increasingly struggle with non-calculator tasks that require symbolic derivation.
    - The use of technology in school has inadvertently replaced "conceptual derivation" with "result verification" (Black-box effect).
    - Transition is now a **"Negotiation of Meaning"** between what students *think* they know (based on grades) and what is *demanded* of them (based on rigor).
- **Contribution**: Documented the "Decline of Algebra" in the modern curriculum and its impact on university STEM retention.

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：Hoyles (2001) 揭示了 **「結構能力的職業門檻 (The Structural Competence Threshold)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：數學轉銜不只是成績的延續，更是 **「職業工具箱 (Professional Toolkit)」** 的重組。Hoyles 指出的「結構能力」缺失，實際上是未來 **「系統化思考者」** 與 **「作業員」** 的分水嶺。在高階職涯（如航太工程、軟體架構、金融建模）中，個體必須具備在「沒有數值數據」的情況下進行「符號化結構預測」的能力。Hoyles 提到的「生涯準備度幻覺」警示我們：如果轉銜支持不包含對「結構化思維」的重建，我們實際上是在把學生送往一個 **「預定失敗的職業未來」**。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未討論如何透過「數位工具的白箱化 (White-boxing)」來修復這種結構能力的斷層。
*   **重構方向**：應將「代數結構感」視為一項核心的 **「職業能動性指標」**。
