## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Universities often address the "gap" in isolating remedial measures rather than taking a multi-stage, systemic institutional approach.
- **Research Problem**: To evaluate the impact of the "McMaster Transition Strategy" involving preparation manuals, diagnostic tests, and curricular redesign.
- **Key Results**: Successful transition requires addressing both technical skills and students' mathematical beliefs. The strategy reduced dropout rates and improved student confidence in first-year calculus.
- **Methodology**: Case study of institutional change at McMaster University; longitudinal tracking of 1st-year cohorts.
