## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: STT difficulties are not just about content knowledge but about the **Change in Rules** of the pedagogical game. Students often bring a "School Contract" (expectation of guidance) to a university setting that operates on a "University Contract" (expectation of autonomy).
- **Research Problem**: How can the construct of the "Didactic Contract" explain the difficulties students face during the STT?
- **Key Results**: The "Rupture" occurs because the **Institutional Habitus** of university math departments values independent struggle, while students expect demonstration. Successful transition requires a **"Re-negotiation of the Contract"** where students explicitly learn that their previous successful school strategies are no longer valid.
- **Methodology**: Qualitative case studies across European universities using Brousseau’s Theory of Didactical Situations (TDS).

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：Pepin (2014) 的「教學契約」變遷，本質上是 **「職業契約 (Professional Contract)」** 的預演。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：中學提供的「受保護契約」讓學生處於依賴型生涯階段。而大學的「自主契約」要求學生展現 **「職業能動性 (Professional Agency)」**——這與真實職場的契約非常相似：沒有人會告訴你所有答案。跨越契約斷裂，就是 **「從學生到專業人」** 的生涯跨越。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：Pepin 討論了契約的協商，但未討論 **「職業願景的激勵作用」**。如果學生將大學視為「進入職業社群的實習期」，他們會更主動地接受「自主契約」的挑戰。
*   **重構方向**：教學契約的協商應與 **「職業素養教育」** 結合，讓學生明白「自主學習」是職業能力的必修課。
