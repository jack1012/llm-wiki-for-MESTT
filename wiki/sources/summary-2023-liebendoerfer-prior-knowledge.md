## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: While prior knowledge is a known predictor, its "fine-grained" impact on specific university topics like Analysis is poorly mapped.
- **Research Problem**: To investigate how specific topic-related prior knowledge from school predicts success in related university calculus modules.
- **Key Results**: Finds that prior knowledge of "Functions" and "Derivatives" predicts university success, but the transition to "Rigorous Analysis" requires a qualitative shift that prior knowledge alone cannot bridge.
- **Methodology**: Quantitative longitudinal study; N=700+; Structural Equation Modeling.
