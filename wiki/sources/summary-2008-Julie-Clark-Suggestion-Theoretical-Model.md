## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Transition research is often fragmented between cognitive, sociological, and institutional lenses. There is a need for a unified theoretical model.
- **Research Problem**: How can we combine Communities of Practice (CoP) and Boundary Crossing concepts to model the STT?
- **Key Results**: Proposes an **Integrative Model** where transition is seen as a **Boundary Crossing** between the Secondary School community and the University Mathematics community. Key roles include **Brokers** (individuals who belong to both and can bridge the gap) and **Boundary Objects** (tasks or artifacts that travel between communities).
- **Methodology**: Theoretical synthesis and review of literature from the Irish and Australian contexts.

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：Clark (2008) 實際上是在討論如何為學生進行 **「專業引薦 (Professional Sponsorship)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：轉銜不只是學分轉換，而是學生的 **「跨國遷徙」**（從中學文化到大學專業文化）。「仲介 (Brokerage)」的角色就是學生的「生涯導航員」。如果大學沒有提供有效的仲介，學生在生涯路徑上就會迷航，產生 **「職業孤立感」**。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：討論了制度橋樑，但較少討論學生個體主動「尋求仲介」的意願。
*   **重構方向**：應將 **[[Brokerage]]** 連結至「生涯社會資本」的累積。
