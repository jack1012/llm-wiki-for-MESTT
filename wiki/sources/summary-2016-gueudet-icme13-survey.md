## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: The "transition problem" is a global phenomenon that cannot be solved by simple modular interventions; it requires a deep understanding of institutional and didactical discontinuities.
- **Research Problem**: To provide a comprehensive review of the state-of-the-art in STT research as presented at ICME-13.
- **Key Results**: Identifies three main axes of research: (1) Cognitive (Modes of thinking), (2) Institutional (Didactical contract), and (3) Sociocultural (Identity and Affect). Emphasizes the need for holistic models.
- **Methodology**: Expert synthesis of international research contributions.
