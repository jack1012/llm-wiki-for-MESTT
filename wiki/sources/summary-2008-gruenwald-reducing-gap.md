## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: The "gap" between school and university mathematics is a global issue where lecturers perceive a decline in students' conceptual understanding and formal reasoning skills, often blaming secondary education.
- **Research Problem**: To systematize the perspectives of 63 international university lecturers on the specific causes of the gap and the effectiveness of current pedagogical remedies across 24 countries.
- **Key Results**: Identifies "Mathematical Maturity" as the core missing element; students rely too much on algorithmic procedures and lack the ability to use formal language or conduct proofs.
- **Methodology**: Survey (Questionnaire with open-ended questions), 63 respondents from 24 countries.
