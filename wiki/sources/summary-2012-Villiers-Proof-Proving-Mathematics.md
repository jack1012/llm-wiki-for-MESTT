## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: The "Proof Gap" is the most severe cognitive barrier in the STT. Secondary schools often emphasize empirical or visual evidence, while universities require formal, axiomatic, and deductive proof, causing a massive "Proof Shock."
- **Research Problem**: How can the mathematics education community bridge the gap between intuitive understanding and formal rigorous proving?
- **Key Results**: Defines proof as serving multiple functions: **Verification** (is it true?) and **Explanation** (why is it true?). Successful transition requires students to move beyond "Empirical Proof Schemes" (based on examples) to "Deductive Proof Schemes." Technology (DGS/CAS) can facilitate this by allowing for "Experimental Mathematics," but only if guided toward formal justification.
- **Methodology**: Synthesis of international research contributions; comparative curriculum analysis; cognitive modeling of proof schemes.

## Structured Reading Notes
- **Theoretical Framework**: Harel and Sowder's Proof Schemes; Brousseau's Theory of Didactic Situations (TDS).
- **Core Concepts**: [[Proof as Explanation]], [[Proof Schemes]], [[Local Proof]], [[Experimental Mathematics]], [[The Proof Gap]].
- **Main Findings**: 
    - Students often perform "Ritual Proof" to satisfy the teacher without grasping the logical necessity.
    - The shift to university requires an **"Epistemic Transition"**: from seeing math as "calculating" to seeing it as "arguing."
    - Visual evidence (DGS) can be a powerful "bridge" but can also become a "crutch" that delays formalization.
- **Contribution**: Established a multi-dimensional framework for understanding and teaching proof across the K-16 continuum.

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：Hanna & de Villiers (2012) 揭露了數學專業中的 **「知識論代理權 (Epistemic Agency)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：學會「構造證明」不只是學會一個公式，而是獲得了 **「定義真實 (Defining Truth)」** 的職業權力。在低階職涯中，個體只需「遵循程序」；但在高階研究或決策職位中，個體必須能夠 **「證明其邏輯的嚴密性」**。轉銜中的「證明衝擊」實際上是學生第一次面臨 **「專業誠信 (Professional Integrity)」** 的考驗——你不能只說它是對的，你必須證明它是對的。這種從「經驗者」到「論證者」的轉變，是通往高級學術與技術生涯的 **「成人禮 (Rites of Passage)」**。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未深入討論「證明能力」在跨學科領域（如法學、計算機科學）中的職業遷移價值。
*   **重構方向**：應將「證明」定位為一項 **「頂級職涯溝通工具」**，用於在充滿不確定性的專業世界中建立共識。
