## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: With the massification of higher education, the number of students with Learning Disabilities (LD) entering university has surged. However, institutional support remains trapped in a K-12 "academic remediation" mindset, ignoring the unique psychosocial and career transition needs of young adults.
- **Research Problem**: What is the current state of school adjustment for LD college students in Taiwan, and what background factors predict their success or failure in the STT phase?
- **Key Results**: (1) **The Career Information Gap**: While LD students report positive interpersonal relationships, their highest unmet need is not academic tutoring, but "Career Development Information." They suffer from severe anxiety regarding their future employability. (2) **The Early Identification Dividend**: Students diagnosed with LD before elementary school show significantly better adjustment in college compared to those diagnosed in junior high. Early identification builds long-term psychological capital. (3) **The Cultural Shift in Resilience**: Unlike Western LD students who succeed by "bypassing weaknesses," Taiwanese LD students emphasize "facing difficulties with effort" (努力面對), aligning with the broader Confucian Effort Belief.
- **Methodology**: Quantitative survey research analyzing demographics, self-concept, success attributes, and adjustment outcomes.

## Structured Reading Notes (中文摘要)
- **研究主題**: 台灣學障大專生之學校生活適應與資源需求分析。
- **核心發現**:
    - **預期性職涯焦慮**: 學障生在大學面臨的最大挑戰並非當下的學業當鋪，而是對未來職場接納度的恐懼。他們對「職涯探索資源」的渴求遠超過校方預期。
    - **早期鑑定的複利效應**: 越早確認學障身分，學生越能提早完成「障礙認同」的心理建設，進而在大學階段展現出更高的適應韌性。
    - **特教資源的錯位**: 現行大學資源教室多著重於「課業輔導」與「行政減免」，缺乏將學障生引導至「勞動市場對接」的系統性支持。
- **貢獻**: 將學障研究的視角從「學術補救」拉升至「職涯平權與心理資本建構」。

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：王瓊珠 (2017) 揭示了特殊群體轉銜中的 **「隱形天花板：學障生的預期性職業效能焦慮 (The Invisible Ceiling of Transition: Anticipatory Career Efficacy Anxiety in LD Students)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：王教授的研究打破了我們對學障生的刻板印象。在 STT 轉銜階段，學障生的核心危機已經從「學業生存」轉移到了 **「職業效能驗證 (Verification of Career Efficacy)」**。研究數據顯示，他們最缺乏的是「職涯資訊」，這反映了他們比一般學生更早、更深刻地感受到社會篩選的殘酷性。他們面臨的是一種 **「預期性焦慮 (Anticipatory Anxiety)」**：未來的職場會不會因為我的閱讀速度慢或書寫困難而拒絕我？這種焦慮會導致他們在轉銜時採取極端保守的防禦策略。對於這群學生，大學的轉銜輔導不能僅停留在「發放輔具」或「延長考試時間」的行政恩賜，而必須轉向 **「職涯平權的培力 (Career Equity Empowerment)」**，幫助他們將「障礙特質」轉化為工作場域中的「替代性優勢」。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未探討「數位科技輔具 (Assistive Technology)」的熟練度如何成為學障生弭平職涯落差的核心武器。
*   **重構方向**：應將「早期鑑定」視為建立學生 **「終身職涯韌性 (Lifelong Career Resilience)」** 的起點。
