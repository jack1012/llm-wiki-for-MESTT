## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Even with high school success, students experience "Culture Shock" when faced with the speed, abstraction, and autonomy required at university.
- **Research Problem**: To capture the "student voice" regarding the most jarring aspects of the transition in the first year.
- **Key Results**: Students identify the "Language of Mathematics" (formal notation) and the "Pace of Lectures" as the primary shocks. Feeling like a "small fish in a big pond" contributes to affective distress.
- **Methodology**: Analysis of student questionnaires and focus groups (N=150+).
