## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: The transition from school to university is often treated as a curriculum issue, but it is deeply affected by differences in **Teaching Styles** and **Assessment Practices** that remain invisible to both sectors.
- **Research Problem**: How do the expectations and pedagogical cultures of secondary teachers and tertiary lecturers differ, and how does this affect student motivation?
- **Key Results**: Identifies a significant **Communication Gap**. Secondary teaching is highly structured and "teacher-centered," while tertiary environments demand a high degree of **Independent Learning** for which students are unprepared. A major finding is the **Decline in Academic Confidence** during the first semester, as students struggle with the shift in responsibility. The study argues for "Forging Links"—creating active dialogue between sectors to align expectations.
- **Methodology**: Mixed-methods; 2-year longitudinal study; surveys and interviews with teachers, lecturers, and students in New Zealand.

## Structured Reading Notes
- **Theoretical Framework**: Institutional Discontinuity; Motivation Theory.

- **Main Findings**: 
    - Secondary teachers often "teach to the test," reducing mathematics to procedural mastery.
    - University assessment is more flexible but also more rigorous, creating "Assessment Shock."
    - Technology is perceived as more important in university than it actually is in practice, leading to misplaced student anxiety.
- **Contribution**: Highlighted the emotional and cultural dimensions of the transition beyond cognitive content.

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：Thomas (2012) 揭示了 **「職業自信心的脆裂 (Fragility of Professional Confidence)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：學生的自信心下滑，實質上是其 **「職業角色原型 (Professional Role Prototype)」** 的崩潰。在中學，他們是「成功的學生」；在大學，他們突然變成了「迷茫的新手」。如果缺乏 **「獨立學習能力」**，他們在面對未來快速變遷的職涯環境時，將失去核心的 **「職涯能動性」**。轉銜教育的核心應是鍛造學生的 **「自主性 (Autonomy)」**，讓他們從「被動接收者」成長為「自我驅動的專業人才」。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未討論如何利用「同儕支持網絡」來緩解這種自信心的集體下滑。
*   **重構方向**：應將「獨立學習能力」明確標註為一項 **「終身職涯資產」**。
