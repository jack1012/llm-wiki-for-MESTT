## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Graduate student attrition is under-researched compared to undergraduate dropout, yet graduate-level non-completion has distinct mechanisms tied to career-study conflict and thesis burden — not simply academic failure.
- **Research Problem**: What are the most important variables predicting graduate student attrition patterns in Taiwan, and how do institutional characteristics (public/private, program type) moderate these patterns?
- **Key Results**:
  1. **Work Demand is the #1 Variable** (by Random Forest importance via misclassification rate/permutation): "Work Demand" outranks all academic factors in predicting which cluster a university belongs to.
  2. **Variable Importance Ranking**: Work Demand > Others > Illness/Injury > Academic/Exam/Thesis > Interest Mismatch.
  3. **Financial Factors Are Surprisingly Minor**: Economic/financial difficulty is among the **least important** predictors for master's level attrition — contrasting with undergraduate-level dropout where finances are central.
  4. **Institutional Variability is High**: Dropout patterns vary significantly by: (a) Public vs. Private schools; (b) Day vs. Professional master's programs.
  5. **Attrition Increases with Degree Level**: PhD (21%) > Professional Master's (16%) > Day Master's (14%) — suggesting that **duration and thesis dependency** compound attrition risk.
- **Methodology**: Cluster analysis + Random Forest (variable importance via permutation/misclassification rate) using 107-108 Academic Year data from the MOE IR platform across general universities.
- **Theoretical Framework**: Tinto (1975, 1993) Student Integration Model; Bean (1980) Student Attrition Model; Lovitts (2001) Graduate Attrition Framework.

## Structured Reading Notes (中文摘要)
- **研究主題**: 使用隨機森林方法分析影響台灣大學校院碩士班學生休學之最關鍵因素。
- **核心發現**:
    - **工作需求最重要**: 以 Random Forest 排序後，「工作需求」是預測學校集群差異的最重要變數，遠超學術因素。
    - **財務因素次要**: 碩士生層次的財務困難重要性遠低於大學生，說明研究所退場的機制根本不同。
    - **公私立差異顯著**: 不同學校屬性呈現明顯的退場原因分佈差異。
- **貢獻**: 首次以 Random Forest 方法系統性地確認「工作需求」為碩士生退場的核心驅動力，同時排除了財務因素的主導地位。

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：林靜慧 (2021b) 從統計層面確認了 **「生涯-學業衝突 (Career-Study Conflict)」** 的制度根源，並以 Random Forest 方法排除了財務解釋，將問題明確指向「職場結構」。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：工作需求主導的退場模式揭示了台灣職場的結構矛盾：雇主期待員工擁有更高學歷，卻同時施加超載的工作負荷，使在職進修者陷入「學業與職涯的雙重消耗」。這是典型的 **「系統性職涯陷阱 (Systemic Career Trap)」**。
*   **Tinto 框架的在地驗證**: 工作需求（外部系統整合壓力）> 學術整合（內部整合），在碩士生身上呈現出與大學生不同的 Tinto 模型動態。
*   **重構方向**: 碩士生退場政策的核心應聚焦於「彈性學制設計」（學分制、線上化、延修）而非財務補助，以解決職涯-學業衝突的根本結構。
