## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Traditional tutoring often focuses on correcting errors, which can damage students' self-efficacy during the critical first semester.
- **Research Problem**: To evaluate a peer-support intervention designed to develop "Mathematical Resilience" through coached problem-solving.
- **Key Results**: Finds that resilience-focused coaching reduces anxiety and improves students' ability to work through "stuck" moments in proof-based tasks.
- **Methodology**: Action research; evaluation of a "Resilience Support" pilot project at a HE institution.
