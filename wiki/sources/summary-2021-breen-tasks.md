## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: There is a mismatch between the procedural focus of Irish secondary education (Leaving Certificate) and the conceptual rigor required at the university level.
- **Research Problem**: What are students' views on the differences between school and university math? How do specific tasks influence these views?
- **Key Results**: Students transition from "finding the answer" (procedural) to "understanding the principles" (relational). Non-routine tasks are pivotal in signaling this shift.
- **Methodology**: Qualitative semi-structured interviews with 18 students from two Irish universities, focusing on their experiences with Differential Calculus modules.
