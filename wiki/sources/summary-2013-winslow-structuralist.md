## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: The shift from school to university mathematics is not just a change in content but a "structuralist" regime change that students often fail to notice until it's too late.
- **Research Problem**: To analyze the transition via the Anthropological Theory of Didactic (ATD), focusing on the shift from "calculus" praxeologies to "structuralist" (formal) praxeologies.
- **Key Results**: Identifies a "praxeological gap" where university tasks require a theoretical MILIEU that is absent in secondary school. The "Double Discontinuity" persists because teacher education fails to bridge these praxeologies.
- **Methodology**: Theoretical analysis based on ATD (Task, Technique, Technology, Theory).
