## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Mathematical interest is not a stable trait during the transition; its decline is a major factor in first-year attrition.
- **Research Problem**: To track the longitudinal development of student interest in both school-related and university-related mathematics.
- **Key Results**: Finds a significant decline in *university-related* interest in the first 6-8 weeks. Students who manage to maintain interest are those who successfully shift their "Concept of Mathematics".
- **Methodology**: Quantitative longitudinal study; N=500+; Latent Growth Curve Modeling.
