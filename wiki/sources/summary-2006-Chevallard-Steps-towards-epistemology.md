## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Mathematics is often taught as a set of isolated, "monumental" facts (The Monumentalism of knowledge), which prevents students from understanding the *reason* for mathematical activity.
- **Research Problem**: How can we model mathematical knowledge not as internal cognition, but as a social and institutional **Human Practice**?
- **Key Results**: Introduces the **Praxeology** framework, composed of four elements: (1) **Task** (the problem); (2) **Technique** (how to solve it); (3) **Technology** (the rational justification for the technique); and (4) **Theory** (the overarching logical system). In the STT, the "Technique" learned in school often lacks the "Technology" and "Theory" required at university. This causes an **Institutional Rupture**: students arrive with "tools" but no "reasoning," while university demands "reasoning" for "tools" students haven't mastered yet.
- **Methodology**: Epistemological and anthropological analysis of mathematical activity within institutions.

## Structured Reading Notes
- **Theoretical Framework**: Anthropological Theory of the Didactic (ATD).
- **Core Concepts**: [[Praxeology (T-t-theta-Theta)]], [[Institutional Ecology]], [[The Monumentalism of Knowledge]], [[Didactical Transposition]].
- **Main Findings**: 
    - Knowledge is an **"Inhabitant"** of a specific institution; it changes when it moves from one institution to another (Transposition).
    - Transition success depends on the student's ability to adapt their **Praxeology** to the new **Institutional Ecology**.
    - The "Research and Study Path" (RSP) is proposed as a way to replace the "Monumental" teaching style with a question-driven approach.
- **Contribution**: Provided a structural tool to analyze the content-specific gaps in the transition.

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：Chevallard (2006) 揭示了轉銜是一場 **「職業生態系的演化 (Evolution of the Professional Ecology)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：Chevallard 告訴我們，數學知識不是抽象的，而是 **「制度性的實踐」**。轉銜困難實質上是 **「職業生態系 (Institutional Ecology)」** 的變遷。學生在中學掌握的「生存技術 (Technique)」在大學的「理論 (Theory)」環境中失效。這種 **「實踐活動 (Praxeology) 的斷裂」** 導致了嚴重的 **「職業無能感」**。成功的學生是那些能在新的制度環境中，迅速將「技術」與「理論」進行對齊的人。轉銜本質上是 **「職業實踐標準的重啟」**。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：過於強調制度結構，忽略了個體在面對「生態系改變」時的情緒調節能力。
*   **重構方向**：應將「Praxeology 的完整性」標註為衡量 **「專業成熟度」** 的核心指標。
