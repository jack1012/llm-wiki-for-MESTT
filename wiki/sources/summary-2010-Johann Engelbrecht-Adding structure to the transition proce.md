## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: The school-to-university transition is often "traumatic" because school mathematics focuses on "doing" (calculations), while university mathematics demands "thinking" (logic, rigor, and formal communication).
- **Research Problem**: How can university departments "structure" the transition to help students move toward advanced mathematical activity?
- **Key Results**: Proposes a **Two-Step Learning Model**: (1) Internalized Understanding and (2) Formal Communication. The study finds that students often have a "Concept Image" (intuitive grasp) but fail because they cannot navigate the **"Communication Gap"**—the inability to express their thoughts through formal, rigorous mathematical language. Success in the STT depends on the transition from a **Technical Operator** (solving equations) to a **Professional Analyst** (building logical arguments).
- **Methodology**: Theoretical analysis and review of first-year mathematics pedagogy.

    - Departments must explicitly teach "mathematical writing" as a new form of professional literacy.
- **Contribution**: Defined the transition as a shift in **Communicative Competence**.

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：Engelbrecht (2010) 揭示了轉銜作為 **「專業溝通者的身分建構 (Identity Construction of the Professional Communicator)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：在現代職涯中，單純「算出結果」已不足夠，更核心的價值在於「論證與溝通為什麼這個結果是正確的」。Engelbrecht 指出的「從做到想」的轉變，實質上是從 **「技術操作員 (Technical Operator)」** 到 **「專業分析師 (Professional Analyst)」** 的 **「職業身份轉向」**。轉銜中的「溝通鴻溝」本質上是 **「職業敘事力 (Vocational Narrative Ability)」** 的缺失。如果學生無法學會這種專業語言，他們將在未來的高階職場中面臨 **「專業失語症」**，無法參與具備高回報率的決策過程。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未討論多媒體或協作工具如何幫助學生跨越這種論證上的溝通障礙。
*   **重構方向**：應將「邏輯論證能力」標註為衡量 **「職業主體性」** 的核心指標。
