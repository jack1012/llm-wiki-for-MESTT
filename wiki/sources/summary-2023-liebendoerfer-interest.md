## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P3, P4：興趣軌跡與情意發展
- **Research Problem**: How do student interest profiles and trajectories change during the STT, and what patterns of interest development characterize successful versus at-risk groups in university mathematics?
- **Key Results**: Person-centered cluster analysis reveals distinct interest trajectory profiles during STT (maintaining, declining, recovering); successful students show sustained or recovering interest patterns; interest development is partially independent of achievement, revealing affective processes distinct from cognitive performance; critical periods for intervention emerge where interest loss accelerates.
- **Methodology**: Quantitative person-centered approach; longitudinal cluster analysis; latent profile analysis of interest trajectories; comparative analysis of interest patterns across achievement groups.
