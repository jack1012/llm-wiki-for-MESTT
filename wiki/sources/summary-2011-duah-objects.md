## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P1：認知斷裂問題
- **Research Problem**: How do shifts in the reification and abstraction of mathematical objects (from procedural to formal) affect student understanding and cognitive engagement in the STT?
- **Key Results**: The reification of mathematical objects from procedural actions to abstract entities is fundamental to STT; students struggle with "dual process" objects that require simultaneous procedural and structural understanding; formal proof demands cognitive shifts in how objects are conceptualized.
- **Methodology**: Qualitative case study analysis; interviews with students; analysis of mathematical object development through procedural-object and structural-object paradigms; learning trajectory analysis.
