## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Students entering university mathematics exhibit a high level of procedural fluency but lack the ability to apply this knowledge to non-routine or contextual problems.
- **Research Problem**: What are the most critical gaps in freshman mathematical ability as perceived by university lecturers globally?
- **Key Results**: Identifies the **"Formulation Hurdle"** as the primary cognitive barrier. Students can solve an equation if given, but they fail at **Mathematical Modelling**—the step of translating a real-world description into a mathematical structure. The study reveals a **Mechanical Survival Bias**: students use rote-learned algorithms to pass school exams, creating a "veneer of competence" that shatters when faced with the abstract, conceptual demands of tertiary mathematics.
- **Methodology**: International survey (N=150 lecturers); thematic analysis of open-ended responses.

## Structured Reading Notes
- **Theoretical Framework**: Practitioner Inquiry; Cognitive Gap Analysis.
- **Core Concepts**: [[The Formulation Hurdle]], [[Mechanical Survival Bias]], [[Mathematical Maturity]], [[Didactic Transposition]].
- **Main Findings**: 
    - The gap is not in the "content" (topics covered) but in the **"Depth of Understanding"**.
    - Lecturers believe secondary schools focus too much on "Calculator-friendly" tasks rather than "Thinker-demanding" tasks.
    - Modelling is seen as the "missing bridge" between school math and professional math.
- **Contribution**: Quantified the **"Formulation Step"** as the decisive threshold for transition success.

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：Klymchuk (2011) 揭示了 **「運算勞工與專業決策者 (Calculators vs. Decision-Makers)」** 的分水嶺。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：文中的「建模困難」本質上是 **「職業主體性 (Professional Agency)」** 的缺失。在現代職場（如 AI 工程、量化金融、精算），純粹的計算已由機器完成；真正的專業價值在於 **「問題定義與模型建構」**。那些被困在「機械生存偏誤」中的學生，在生涯發展上極易淪為被淘汰的「運算工具」，而非具備創見的「系統架構師」。轉銜教育的核心應是將學生從「公式追隨者」轉變為 **「意義建構者」**。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未討論如何透過「專案式學習 (PBL)」在轉銜期修復這種建模斷層。
*   **重構方向**：應將「建模能力」標註為衡量 **「職業成熟度 (Career Maturity)」** 的核心指標。
