## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P3, P1：身分建構與認知-情意整合
- **Research Problem**: How can personal journaling be used as a metacognitive tool to investigate and understand the nuanced experiences of first-year architecture students during their STT?
- **Key Results**: Text analysis of student journals using AntConc confirmed five prevalent transition themes
- **Methodology**: Qualitative text analysis (corpus analysis via AntConc) of personal journals submitted by 61 first-year Architecture students at Politecnico di Milano.
