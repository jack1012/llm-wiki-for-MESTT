## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P5：理論碎片化問題
- **Research Problem**: How can a unified four-planes theoretical model integrate cognitive, affective, institutional, and sociocultural perspectives to provide a holistic understanding of the secondary-tertiary transition in mathematics?
- **Key Results**: A four-planes model (cognitive, affective, institutional, sociocultural) synthesizes fragmented STT research into a unified framework; the model reveals how cognitive and affective discontinuities interact with institutional constraints; holistic interventions address multiple planes simultaneously.
- **Methodology**: Theoretical synthesis and framework development; systematic integration of cognitive, affective, institutional, and sociocultural literature; meta-theoretical analysis of STT research landscape.
