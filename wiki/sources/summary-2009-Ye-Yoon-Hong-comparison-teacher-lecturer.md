## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: The transition from school to university is often viewed as a student-centered problem, but it is structurally driven by a **Cognitive Disconnect** between those who teach at each level.
- **Research Problem**: How do secondary school teachers and university lecturers differ in their perceptions of mathematical rigor, technology, and student readiness?
- **Key Results**: Identifies a massive **Expectation Gap**. Teachers overestimate students' conceptual readiness and underestimate the jump in **Formal Rigor** required at university. Lecturers, conversely, are often unaware of the modern pedagogical shifts in schools (e.g., modeling and collaborative learning). This creates a situation where students are "abandoned" between two inconsistent professional standards.
- **Methodology**: Survey and interview-based study of math educators in New Zealand; comparative analysis of two professional sectors.

## Structured Reading Notes
- **Theoretical Framework**: Institutional Transition Theory; Professional Socialization.
- **Core Concepts**: [[Cognitive Misalignment]], [[The Rigor Gap]], [[Professional Silos]], [[Institutional Alignment]].
- **Main Findings**: 
    - Teachers and lecturers operate in **"Professional Silos"** with almost zero inter-sectoral communication.
    - The use of technology is inconsistent and lacks a unified pedagogical philosophy across the transition.
    - Teachers focus on "achieving the grade," while lecturers focus on "mastering the system."
- **Contribution**: Demonstrated that the transition crisis is a **Systemic Communication Failure**, not just a student ability failure.

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：Hong (2009) 揭示了轉銜中的 **「專業社會化真空 (Socialization Vacuum)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：學生在 STT 中實際上是在經歷 **「職業身份的震盪」**。中學老師將他們塑造為「考試能手」，而大學講師則要求他們成為「獨立研究者」。這種 **「職業標準的不一致 (Inconsistent Professional Standards)」** 讓學生感到困惑。成功的轉銜需要將「學科老師」與「大學導師」進行 **「職涯目標對齊 (Career Goal Alignment)」**。Hong 提到的「生涯顧問 (Career Advisors)」的角色至關重要：他們應該是跨越這兩個專業世界的 **「翻譯者」**，幫助學生理解中學數學與大學職涯之間的真實連結。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未討論如何建立常態性的「中學-大學老師交流論壇」。
*   **重構方向**：應將「跨部門溝通」視為一項 **「職涯支持系統 (Career Support System)」** 的核心組成。
