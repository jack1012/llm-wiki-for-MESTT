## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: The fragmentation of theories in mathematics education makes it difficult to synthesize research. There is a need for "networking" to understand how different "lenses" shape what we see.
- **Research Problem**: To explore the "Networking of Theories" approach (connecting, coordinating, integrating) to study the secondary-tertiary transition.
- **Key Results**: TDS excels at granular interaction and the "Milieu," while ATD excels at identifying institutional constraints. Demonstrates that combining frameworks providing richer analysis of the didactical contract.
- **Methodology**: Theoretical comparison and cross-analysis of classroom episodes.
