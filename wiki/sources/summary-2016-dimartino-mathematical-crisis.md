## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: The transition to university is often experienced as a "mathematical crisis" where even high-attaining school students suddenly face failure, leading to a collapse of identity.
- **Research Problem**: To analyze the nature of the "mathematical crisis" in STT through the lens of student narratives and identify how perceptions of competence and view of math interact.
- **Key Results**: The crisis is a "rite of passage" characterized by a shift from procedural success to conceptual confusion. Students often lack the self-regulatory tools to bridge the gap, leading to "Identity Shock".
- **Methodology**: Qualitative analysis of student narratives and "Me and Maths" essays; applied TMA (Three-Dimensional Model of Attitude).
