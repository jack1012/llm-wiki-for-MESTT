## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: The shift from a procedural-focused curriculum to a "Living Math" (9-year Integrated) curriculum created a massive cognitive and content rupture in mathematics education at the high school entry point.
- **Research Problem**: What are the specific facets of the math articulation gap in Taiwan's reform era, and how should pedagogical identity shift to bridge this gap?
- **Key Results**: Identifies a **"Cognitive and Content Triple-Gap"**: (1) **The Logic Gap**: A sudden leap from junior high's "Intuitive/Visual Exploration" to senior high's "Formal Symbolic Deduction." (2) **The Content Void**: Key prerequisites (e.g., polynomial operations, geometric proof) were diluted in junior high, leading to a "Prerequisite Crisis" in Grade 10. (3) **The Identity Gap**: Teachers must transition from "Classroom Authority" to "Learning Facilitator," a move that requires significant psychological recalibration. The study argues that without "Bridging Courses" (銜接教材), the education reform inadvertently creates a **Selection Bias** against students who cannot self-correct.
- **Methodology**: Professional reflection and comparative curriculum analysis (Grade 1-9 vs. Grade 10-12).

## Structured Reading Notes (中文摘要)
- **研究主題**: 台灣教改背景下的數學課程銜接問題剖析。
- **核心發現**:
    - **認知跳躍 (Cognitive Jump)**: 國中階段過度強調「生活化」與「具體操作」，導致學生在高中面對「形式化符號」與「演繹證明」時產生劇烈的排斥感。
    - **課程斷層**: 九年一貫課程將部分核心數學工具（如：數列、多項式運算）移往後段或簡化，造成高一學習者的基礎空洞化。
    - **角色轉置**: 提倡教師應從「教室的國王」轉變為「學習的同伴」，強調師生共同建構意義的重要性。
- **貢獻**: 早期精確指出了台灣教改中「銜接」作為獨立研究命題的重要性。

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：陳大魁 (2002) 揭示了課程斷層如何成為 **「職涯流動的結構性障礙 (Structural Barriers to Professional Mobility)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：數學轉銜的成敗，在台灣社會往往決定了學生的 **「學科標籤」** 與未來的 **「職業分流」**。陳大魁指出的「銜接危機」，本質上是在 **「人為地製造職涯濾鏡」**。那些在轉銜點因「形式演繹」門檻而退卻的學生，並非天賦不足，而是被不對稱的課程設計剝奪了進入 STEM 高階職涯的 **「邏輯門票」**。這種 **「結構性不連續 (Structural Discontinuity)」** 導致了青少年在生涯早期就產生了對科學技術的「自我排除 (Self-exclusion)」，影響了國家的長遠人才佈局。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未討論「家庭社會經濟地位」如何幫助部分學生透過補習系統（Shadow Education）補足這段課程空缺，進而加劇教育不平等。
*   **重構方向**：應將「銜接教材」視為一種 **「職涯平權工具」**。
