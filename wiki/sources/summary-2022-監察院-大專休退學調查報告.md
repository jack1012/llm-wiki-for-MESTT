## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Traditional institutional responses to student attrition focus on academic remedial support, while ignoring the structural barriers that prevent students from recalibrating their career paths within the university system.
- **Research Problem**: How do rigid administrative policies and a passive counseling model contribute to the rising attrition rates among university students in Taiwan?
- **Key Results**: (1) **Administrative Exclusion (行政排擠)**: Rigid rules on department transfers, credit waivers, and division switches act as a "Systemic Punishment" for students who discover misalignment, forcing them to drop out rather than transition within the system. (2) **The Trust Deficit in Counseling**: Despite high institutional reporting of counseling services, actual student utilization remains critically low (4-5%), revealing a profound mismatch between bureaucratic "support" and student "help-seeking" behaviors. (3) **Interest Mismatch as a Crisis of Identity**: Attrition peaks in the first two years, signifying a failure to support students through the "Identity Transformation" phase of STT. (4) **Data Fragmentation**: The lack of integration between MOE's national data and institutional IR systems prevents precise identification of at-risk "Cross-Institutional" drifters.
- **Methodology**: Multi-site institutional visits; Documentary analysis of MOE and university IR data; Semi-structured interviews with academic leaders and counselors.

## Structured Reading Notes (中文摘要)
- **研究主題**: 台灣高專校院學生流失成因之制度性弊病調查。
- **核心發現**:
    - **行政牆與路徑依賴**: 嚴苛的轉系門檻與學分抵免規定，構成了一種「行政排擠」，阻礙了學生在發現「志趣不合」後的自我修正。
    - **被動輔導的盲區**: 學校習慣於「坐等學生求助」，忽略了轉銜初期的高風險群往往處於「求助無力」的孤立狀態。
    - **鋸齒型困境**: 經濟弱勢學生陷入「休學工作、復學再休」的循環，現行學制缺乏對這種非線性學習路徑的支持。
- **貢獻**: 揭示了大學行政體系如何從「支持者」變成了轉銜路徑上的「阻礙者」。

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：監察院 (2022) 揭示了轉銜失敗背後的 **「對職涯路徑修正權的系統性剝奪 (Systemic Deprivation of the Right to Career Recalibration)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：監察院報告最深刻的洞察在於揭露了 **「行政摩擦力」** 對職涯主體性的傷害。當學生在轉銜期勇敢承認「選錯系」並試圖修正時，大學體系往往透過繁瑣的程序與高昂的學分成本進行 **「制度性懲罰」**。這種懲罰機制將「職涯探索」汙名化為「不穩定」，實質上是迫使學生在 **「沈默的忍受」** 與 **「斷裂的離校」** 之間做選擇。對於弱勢學生而言，這種摩擦力會直接導致 **「職涯挫敗感的累積」**，使轉銜變成一場不可逆的損耗。轉銜教育應被重新定義為 **「路徑修正的潤滑劑」**，行政體系必須從「防弊守門人」轉向「職涯導航的賦能者」。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未探討「校際合作」或「開放式學位」是否能從根本上消解這種行政排擠。
*   **重構方向**：應將「轉系成功率」與「學分抵免彈性」標註為衡量 **「大學轉銜韌性」** 的核心人權指標。
