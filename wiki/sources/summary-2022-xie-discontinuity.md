## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: The "double discontinuity" (Felix Klein's concept) manifests in both student transitions to university and pre-service teacher preparation, yet teacher candidates lack experience navigating tertiary discontinuities, compounding pedagogical challenges.
- **Research Problem**: To explore the double discontinuity problem in mathematics pre-service teacher education, examining how prospective teachers experience and understand the secondary-to-tertiary transition and its implications for their future teaching practice.
- **Key Results**: ## 關鍵發見 (Key Findings)
- **Methodology**: Case study of pre-service teachers' transition to university mathematics; Felix Klein framework analysis; Australian higher education context; critical incident analysis.
