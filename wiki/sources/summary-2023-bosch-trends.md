## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P5：理論碎片化問題
- **Research Problem**: What are the emerging trends and innovations in research on the transition to university mathematics, and how do different theoretical frameworks (ATD, SRP, networking) contribute to understanding STT?
- **Key Results**: Multiple theoretical frameworks (ATD, SRP, networking approaches) contribute complementary perspectives on STT; recent innovations focus on institutional reform and collaborative support systems; theory networking reveals conceptual bridges between cognitive and institutional analyses.
- **Methodology**: Systematic literature review and theoretical synthesis; comparative analysis of ATD, SRP, and networking frameworks; meta-theoretical examination of research trends across the decade.
