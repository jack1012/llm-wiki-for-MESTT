## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P1, P7：轉銜任務設計與介入
- **Research Problem**: What is a comprehensive taxonomy of interventions and pedagogical practices designed to support the secondary-tertiary transition in mathematics, and how do different intervention types (curricular, pedagogical, supportive, systemic) address distinct STT challenges?
- **Key Results**: Taxonomy distinguishes between task-design interventions (proof-oriented tasks, bridge-building problems), instructional practices (scaffolding, problem-based learning), support systems (tutoring, bridging courses), and systemic reforms (curriculum coordination); no single intervention addresses all dimensions; effectiveness depends on alignment with institutional context.
- **Methodology**: Systematic taxonomy development; meta-analysis of intervention studies; international comparative analysis of practices across institutions; synthesis of teacher narratives and intervention outcomes.
