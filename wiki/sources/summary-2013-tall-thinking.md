## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P1：認知斷裂問題
- **Research Problem**: How do students transition from procedural arithmetic and symbolic thinking to formal mathematical thinking, and what cognitive obstacles ("met-befores") obstruct this transition at the university level?
- **Key Results**: Met-befores (cognitive obstacles from prior learning) fundamentally block axiomatic-formal thinking; the transition requires cognitive reconstruction, not merely accumulation of knowledge; three-worlds framework explains how students operate in different cognitive modalities.
- **Methodology**: Theoretical analysis and cognitive development framework; synthesis of research on the evolution of mathematical thinking from procedural to formal systems.
