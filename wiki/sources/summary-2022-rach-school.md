## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P4：預測悖論問題
- **Research Problem**: Which school-related variables (prior achievement, beliefs, dispositions, learning approaches) longitudinally predict university mathematics study success and persistence in the STT?
- **Key Results**: School achievement alone is weak predictor of university mathematics success; student beliefs about learning, dispositions toward mathematics, and learning approaches significantly mediate the transition; longitudinal analysis reveals that attitudinal factors evolve differently from achievement factors.
- **Methodology**: Quantitative longitudinal study; measurement of school variables (achievement, beliefs, learning approaches); prediction modeling of university success; mediator analysis using structural equation modeling.
