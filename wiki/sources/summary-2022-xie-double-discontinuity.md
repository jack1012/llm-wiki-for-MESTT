## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Prospective teachers often view university mathematics as irrelevant to their future school teaching, exacerbating the "second discontinuity" in their professional journey.
- **Research Problem**: To investigate the impact of a specific course intervention on prospective teachers' beliefs regarding the relevance of advanced mathematics.
- **Key Results**: Finds that demonstrating "Double Discontinuity" explicitly in teacher training can improve students' beliefs about the coherence between school and university mathematics.
- **Methodology**: Mixed-methods; pre-post survey and qualitative interviews with 80+ prospective teachers.
