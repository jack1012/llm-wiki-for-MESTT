## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P3：情意-身分危機問題
- **Research Problem**: How do students engage in identity work and develop mathematical identities during the STT, and how do sociocultural factors shape whether students position themselves as "mathematics people" at the university level?
- **Key Results**: Mathematical identity work is central to STT success; students actively construct or reject mathematics-identity positions based on sociocultural factors and peer positioning; identity crisis is a normative part of the transition, not pathological.
- **Methodology**: Qualitative sociocultural study; longitudinal interviews with university students; narrative identity analysis; examination of positioning and agency in mathematical communities.
