## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Transition failure is historically analyzed as an individual cognitive deficit. Artigue argues it is primarily an **Institutional Rupture** caused by the clash of two distinct mathematical cultures.
- **Research Problem**: How can the Anthropological Theory of the Didactic (ATD) be used to analyze the "Ruptures" in mathematical practice during the transition?
- **Key Results**: Identifies a fundamental shift from a **"Praxis-led"** school culture (focus on procedures and techniques) to a **"Logos-led"** university culture (focus on theoretical justification and formal proof). The university elevates the **"Logos Block"** (Theory and Technology) from the periphery to the center of evaluation. Students are forced to become **"Institutional Nomads"**—learning to navigate a new territory with a different, often implicit, **Didactic Contract**. Success depends on the ability to reconfigure one's "Praxeology" to meet the new institutional demands.
- **Methodology**: Theoretical synthesis using ATD; comparative review of international transition studies.

## Structured Reading Notes
- **Theoretical Framework**: Anthropological Theory of the Didactic (ATD); Praxeological Analysis.
- **Core Concepts**: [[Praxeology]], [[Institutional Nomadism]], [[The Logos Block]], [[Macro and Micro Ruptures]].
- **Main Findings**: 
    - The transition involves both **Macro-ruptures** (organizational changes) and **Micro-ruptures** (subtle shifts in how tasks are defined).
    - University lecturers often suffer from "expert blind-spot," assuming their institutional norms are universal truths.
    - Transition is a **"Total Social Fact"**, impacting student identity and belonging as much as cognitive understanding.
- **Contribution**: Established the "Institutional" lens as the dominant paradigm for analyzing STT at scale.

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：Artigue (2012) 揭示了轉銜作為 **「專業游牧者的身分重組 (Identity Reconfiguration of the Professional Nomad)」** 的本質。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：學生在轉銜中面臨的制度衝擊，實質上是他們人生中第一次重大的 **「跨組織生涯跳躍」**。學生成為「游牧者」，意味著他們必須具備 **「環境掃描 (Environmental Scanning)」** 與 **「文化解碼 (Cultural Decoding)」** 的能力。成功的轉銜不僅是學會了微積分，更是學會了如何在 **「高模糊性 (High Ambiguity)」** 的專業環境中生存。這種 **「制度適應力 (Institutional Adaptability)」** 是未來職場中 **「跨國/跨領域人才」** 的核心競爭力。轉銜失敗的學生，往往是因為他們試圖在「新的專業領土」上強行使用「舊的行為地圖」。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未討論數位化轉型（如遠距教學）如何重塑了這種「游牧」的地理邊界與難度。
*   **重構方向**：應將「制度解碼能力」標註為衡量 **「職涯勝任力」** 的關鍵先行指標。
