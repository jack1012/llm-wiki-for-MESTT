## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Tertiary mathematics education suffers from high failure rates, often blamed on student "misconceptions." However, the root cause may be the systemic reliance on **Imitative Reasoning** (rote memorization of algorithms).
- **Research Problem**: Why do students who were successful in high school struggle so significantly with non-routine problems at the university level?
- **Key Results**: Identifies that students often engage in **Pseudo-conceptual Thought**: they produce correct answers by imitating previously seen examples without understanding the underlying logic. The study argues that much of the "learning difficulty" is actually a **"Teaching Difficulty"**, where the educational environment allows students to reduce complex tasks to simple algorithms, thereby bypassing the necessary development of **Creative Mathematically-founded Reasoning (CR)**.
- **Methodology**: Theoretical analysis and review of empirical studies on tertiary student reasoning patterns.

## Structured Reading Notes
- **Theoretical Framework**: Lithner's Framework for Mathematical Reasoning (2008).
- **Core Concepts**: [[Imitative Reasoning]], [[Creative Mathematically-founded Reasoning (CR)]], [[Pseudo-conceptual Thought]], [[Complexity Reduction]].
- **Main Findings**: 
    - Students are highly efficient at "skipping the reasoning" by finding algorithmic shortcuts.
    - Transition success requires moving from "Memorised Reasoning" to "Foundational Reasoning."
    - Struggling is a natural part of learning; it only becomes a "difficulty" when students lack the tools to resolve the struggle through logic.
- **Contribution**: Shifted the focus from "what students don't know" to "how students reason (or fail to reason)."

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：Lithner (2011) 揭示了 **「職業自動化 (Professional Automatism)」** 的陷阱。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：在現代職場中，「模仿算法」的工作正快速被 AI 取代。Lithner 指出的「模仿性推理」實際上是在訓練學生成為 **「低階執行者 (Low-level Implementers)」**。轉銜到大學數學的本質，是要求學生發展出 **「高階問題解決力 (High-level Problem Solving)」**，這是通往科學家、工程師等高薪、具備 **「職業能動性 (Career Agency)」** 職位的核心素養。如果學生卡在模仿階段，他在未來的職業生涯中將極易面臨 **「技能過時」** 的風險。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未探討評量制度（如選擇題或考古題）如何強化了學生的模仿動機。
*   **重構方向**：應將「創造性推理」視為一種 **「核心職涯韌性 (Core Career Resilience)」**。
