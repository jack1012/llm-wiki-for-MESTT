## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P7：介入悖論問題
- **Research Problem**: What are the affective and cognitive impacts of different formats of intensive bridging courses on dropout rates and student persistence in university mathematics?
- **Key Results**: Intensive bridging course formats significantly affect affective outcomes (motivation, anxiety, self-efficacy); cognitive gains alone do not prevent dropout; affective support and community integration are critical factors for persistence.
- **Methodology**: Mixed-methods comparative study; pre/post affective and cognitive assessment; comparison of two bridging course formats; analysis of dropout rates and student persistence outcomes.
