## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Dropout rates in German universities, especially in STEM subjects, remain at critically high levels (up to 40%).
- **Research Problem**: To identify the underlying causes of student dropout by analyzing the gap between pre-study expectations and actual study reality.
- **Key Results**: Identifies performance-related difficulties and "Disappointed Expectations" as the primary drivers of dropout. Social and academic integration are crucial early buffers.
- **Methodology**: Large-scale longitudinal survey of former students (dropout cohorts) in Germany.
