## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: The "secondary-tertiary transition" (STT) is a global phenomenon where students face high failure and dropout rates despite previous academic success.
- **Research Problem**: To provide a definitive overview of the theories, methodologies, and key findings in STT research over the last 30 years.
- **Key Results**: Identifies the evolution of the field from purely cognitive (Tall) to sociocultural (Biza/Gueudet) and affective (Di Martino). Emphasizes that "mathematical maturity" is socially constructed.
- **Methodology**: Expert synthesis of international research for the Encyclopedia of Mathematics Education.
