## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Undergraduate mathematics reform often focuses on individual courses, ignoring the systemic "transformation" required in instructional resources and institutional practices.
- **Research Problem**: To develop and validate an Institutional Self-Assessment (ISA) tool for analyzing the "transformation" of teaching practices in community colleges.
- **Key Results**: Identifies critical pillars for transformation: Individual instructor readiness, Curricular alignment, and Institutional support. The ISA tool allows colleges to map their "transformation stage".
- **Methodology**: Content validation study; expert panel and survey of community college leadership in the USA.
