## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: The sudden decline in Taiwan's birth rate starting from the late 1990s is reaching the higher education sector, creating a "Demographic Cliff" (人口懸崖) that will permanently shrink the academic system.
- **Research Problem**: How will the number of university freshmen and graduates change over the next 16 years, and what are the implications for the sustainability of the current higher education model?
- **Key Results**: (1) **The 117 Barrier**: Predicts that by the 117 academic year (2028), the number of freshmen will hit a historic low of approximately 157,000, a massive decrease from the 270,000 recorded in earlier years. (2) **Structural Imbalance**: The decline is disproportionately faster in the vocational sector (技職體系), further marginalizing applied technology education. (3) **The Ripple Effect**: The shrinking student population will lead to a surplus of faculty, closure of departments, and a decline in international research competitiveness unless the system undergoes radical transformation.
- **Methodology**: Cohort-Component Method based on national birth data and historical transition rates.

## Structured Reading Notes (中文摘要)
- **研究主題**: 台灣大專校院長期生源預測分析。
- **核心數據**:
    - **入學人數腰斬**: 預測大一新生人數將在 105 至 117 學年度間急劇下滑，每年減少幅度顯著。
    - **畢業生供給減少**: 預計大學畢業生人數將隨之減少，導致未來勞動力市場的技術人才供給出現結構性短缺。
- **貢獻**: 提前預告了台灣高教從「規模擴張」轉向「精緻化」或「退場重整」的必然性。

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：教育部 (2018b) 揭露了轉銜中的 **「買方市場轉向與過度競爭陷阱 (The Buyer's Market Shift and Over-Competition Trap)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：生源預測揭示了一個職涯弔詭：雖然大學生人數變少了（供給減少），但學生的 **「轉銜壓力」** 並未減輕。相反，由於大學為求生存而產生的激烈競爭，導致了 **「校系品牌通膨」**。學生在「選校不選系」的集體焦慮下，更容易掉入 **「志趣錯位」** 的陷阱。這種「人口懸崖」帶來的轉銜特徵是：大學從「篩選者」變成了「搶奪者」，而學生則在過多的選擇中失去了對職業目標的深度思考。未來的 STT 轉銜將不再是「能否上大學」，而是「能否在崩潰的體系中找到具備實質職涯價值的錨點」。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未討論「境外生 (International Students)」與「高齡學習者 (Lifelong Learners)」是否能有效填補人口懸崖留下的空缺。
*   **重構方向**：應將「生源規模」視為影響 **「學位含金量」** 的結構性變數。
