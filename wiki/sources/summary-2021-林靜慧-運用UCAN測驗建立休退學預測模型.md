## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Taiwanese universities mandate freshmen to complete the UCAN career assessment for administrative compliance, but the resulting data is rarely utilized for proactive student support or retention strategies.
- **Research Problem**: Can the scores and profiles generated by the UCAN assessment (measuring career interests and competencies) serve as reliable predictors for student suspension and dropout?
- **Key Results**: (1) **Career Clarity as a Shield**: Students who score high on "career interest consistency" and have clear occupational goals upon entry show significantly lower dropout rates. (2) **The Predictive Power of Assessment**: By integrating UCAN data with existing academic records (GPA, attendance), the predictive model's accuracy for identifying at-risk students improved substantially. (3) **Proactive Transformation**: The study demonstrates how to transform UCAN from a "static psychological test" into a "dynamic early-warning radar," enabling counselors to target students suffering from "Interest Mismatch" before they fail academically.
- **Methodology**: Quantitative predictive modeling (e.g., Logistic Regression/Decision Trees) using institutional UCAN assessment databases and student persistence records.

## Structured Reading Notes (中文摘要)
- **研究主題**: 運用大專校院就業職能平台 (UCAN) 數據預測學生休退學風險。
- **核心發現**:
    - **活化沉睡數據**: UCAN 不應只是新生入學的例行公事。測驗中呈現的「職涯興趣廣泛且無定錨」或「興趣與就讀科系嚴重不符」，是極具價值的預警訊號。
    - **雙重維度預測**: 結合「學術表現（成績）」與「職涯定向（UCAN）」雙指標的預警模型，比單看成績能更早找出潛在的流失群體。
    - **精準干預**: 允許資源教室與系所導師針對 UCAN 顯示高風險的學生，主動進行職涯諮商，而非等待學期末的二一退學。
- **貢獻**: 將國家級的職涯測評工具成功轉化為校內的留存防禦機制。

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：林靜慧 (2021) 揭示了轉銜中的 **「數據驅動的職涯對位：UCAN 作為預期性錯位的測謊機 (Data-Driven Career Alignment: UCAN as a Polygraph for Anticipatory Mismatch)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：結合林永安 (2018) 所說的「錯位幻覺」，我們知道學生口頭報告的「志趣不合」往往不準確。而林靜慧的研究提供了解決方案：透過 UCAN 的量表，我們可以客觀地測量學生的 **「職涯定向感 (Career Orientation)」**。當學生在測驗中表現出對未來職業的極度迷惘，或是其高分興趣類型與目前科系毫無交集時，這就是 **「預期性錯位 (Anticipatory Mismatch)」** 的客觀證據。這種模型將轉銜輔導從「災後重建（退學後輔導）」提升到了「災前預防」。它證明了：**職涯探索的不足，就是退學的最大前置風險**。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未探討學生在填寫 UCAN 時是否會因為「行政強迫」而產生隨意作答的「數據噪音」，從而影響預測模型的準確度。
*   **重構方向**：應將「UCAN 職涯興趣一致性」標註為衡量大一新生 **「轉銜心理資本」** 的量化指標。
