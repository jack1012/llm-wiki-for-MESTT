## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Transition research often focuses on "failure" and "deficits", ignoring the positive aspects of students' choices and identity construction.
- **Research Problem**: To investigate how students' "mathematical identity" is constructed through the choice to study mathematics at university.
- **Key Results**: Finds that students view the transition as a personal challenge and an opportunity for growth. Choosing math is an "identity project" where students seek to prove their competence.
- **Methodology**: Narrative analysis; longitudinal interviews with students across the STT.
