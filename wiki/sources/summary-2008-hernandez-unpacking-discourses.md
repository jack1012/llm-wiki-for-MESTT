## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P3：情意-身分危機問題
- **Research Problem**: How do different types of mathematical and educational discourses influence students' decisions to persist or withdraw during the **Secondary-Tertiary Transition (STT)**?
- **Key Results**: The study conceptualizes transition as a **Rite of Passage**. It identifies competing discourses (e.g., mathematics as a "tool" vs. mathematics as "truth") that shape **Identity Construction**. Students who find a "Narrative Identity" that aligns with university mathematical practices are more likely to succeed.
- **Methodology**: Qualitative narrative study; discourse analysis; interviews and focus groups with students undergoing STT; thematic analysis of competing mathematical and educational discourses.
