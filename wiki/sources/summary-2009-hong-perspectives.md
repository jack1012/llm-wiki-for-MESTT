## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P2, P6：制度碰撞與教師盲區
- **Research Problem**: What are secondary calculus teachers' perceptions of the differences between school and university mathematics teaching, and how does this affect student transition?
- **Key Results**: Over 70% of surveyed teachers responded "don't know" when asked about differences in tertiary teaching emphasis. Teachers who did comment noted a shift towards theoretical formalization and larger class sizes at universities, while heavily acknowledging their own focus is strictly geared towards passing the national NCEA exams.
- **Methodology**: Survey (N=178) of secondary calculus teachers in New Zealand, supplemented by interviews.
