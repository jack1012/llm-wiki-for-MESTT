## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Students struggle with the "axiomatic-formal" world of university mathematics because their "embodied" and "symbolic" experiences from school are no longer sufficient for rigorous proof.
- **Research Problem**: To analyze the cognitive development required for the transition from intuitive to formal mathematical thinking.
- **Key Results**: Highlights the role of "Property-based" vs. "Definition-based" deduction. Success requires moving from *describing* objects to *defining* objects through axioms.
- **Methodology**: Theoretical synthesis based on the "Three Worlds" framework.
