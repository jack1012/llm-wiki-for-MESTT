## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Students often transition into university with "patterns" of misconceptions built upon valid secondary experiences that paradoxically become obstacles in formal systems.
- **Research Problem**: To analyze the cognitive discontinuities in the transition from intuitive to formal mathematics in calculus and analysis.
- **Key Results**: Identifies the "Proceptual Divide" where students who rely on fixed procedures (procedural) fail compared to those who can see symbols as flexible mental objects (procepts). The "Concept Image" often conflicts with "Concept Definition".
- **Methodology**: Theoretical synthesis and qualitative analysis of student proof attempts.
