## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Educational interventions often focus on academic grades as the sole predictor of dropout, ignoring the critical role of social integration and institutional commitment.
- **Research Problem**: How can Tinto’s Longitudinal Model be re-contextualized to understand the "hidden" drivers of student withdrawal beyond academic achievement?
- **Key Results**: (1) **Dropout as Social Suicide**: Adopting Durkheim’s perspective, the study views dropout as a form of social suicide within the school community, caused by a failure of integration. (2) **The Social-Academic Interplay**: Persistence is driven by two systems: the Academic (grades/intellectual development) and the Social (peer/faculty interactions). (3) **Institutional Commitment**: Finds that a student’s sense of "belonging" can outweigh academic failure. Students with high social integration may persist despite poor grades, while "socially isolated" high achievers are at high risk of withdrawal. (4) **Pedagogical Re-socialization**: Transition is defined as a process of learning the "hidden curriculum" and social norms of the new institution.

## Structured Reading Notes (中文摘要)
- **研究主題**: 探討影響學生中途離校的非學業因素。
- **核心發現**:
    - **社交自殺隱喻**: 輟學被視為個體與學校「微型社會」的斷裂。當學生感到被邊緣化或價值觀與制度衝突時，會產生「社交自願撤離」。
    - **承諾勝於成就**: 「目標承諾」與「制度承諾」是穩定轉銜的核心。如果學生看不到學業與未來職涯的連結，即便成績優異也會選擇離校。
    - **教學再社會化**: 轉銜不只是知識的累積，更是身份的重組。學生必須學會新的「學術語言」與「社交規範」才能生存。
- **貢獻**: 將西方經典模型與東亞教育環境中的「歸屬感」議題進行了深度連結。

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：何文略 (2022) 揭示了轉銜失敗作為 **「輟學作為社交自殺：職涯脫鉤的本質 (Dropout as Social Suicide: The Essence of Vocational Detachment)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：何文略提醒我們，學生對學校的「承諾」本質上是對未來身分的 **「預先投資 (Anticipatory Investment)」**。當學生在轉銜期感到「社交孤立」或看不到學業對未來職涯的價值時，他會選擇 **「職涯撤資 (Vocational Divestment)」** 並中途離校。這種「社交自殺」背後是 **「職業主體性」** 的崩潰。如果學生在新的學術社群中找不到「回饋感」，他對未來的 **「職業願景 (Vocational Vision)」** 就會模糊，進而導致職涯發展的長期中斷。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未深入探討「職業願景」是否能作為「社交孤立」的補償機制（即：雖然在校沒朋友，但為了職業夢想而忍耐並成功轉銜）。
*   **重構方向**：應將「制度歸屬感」標註為衡量 **「職涯持續力」** 的核心情感指標。
