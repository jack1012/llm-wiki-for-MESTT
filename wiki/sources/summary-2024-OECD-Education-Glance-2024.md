## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Despite global efforts, gender gaps in STEM (especially Mathematics and Engineering) remain rigid and show slow improvement at higher degree levels.
- **Key Results**: While women are now more likely to enter university than men, they are significantly less likely to choose or persist in math-intensive fields. The dropout rate for women in Engineering/Math is higher in the first year than for men in many countries.
- **Contribution**: Provides the most up-to-date baseline for gender-based STT analysis.
