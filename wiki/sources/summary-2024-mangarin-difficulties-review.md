## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Theoretical fragmentation in learning difficulties research hinders practitioners from seeing the interconnectedness of cognitive and affective barriers.
- **Research Problem**: What are the primary bundles of difficulties encountered by students in learning mathematics at various levels?
- **Key Results**: Identifies four major clusters: (1) Conceptual/Cognitive barriers (shallow grasp); (2) [[Math Anxiety]] (vicious cycles of failure); (3) Ineffective traditional methods (redundant rote practice); (4) Socio-cultural resource gaps. Highlights growth mindsets as a key counteractant.
- **Methodology**: Systematic literature review of qualitative and quantitative studies mapping global math difficulty trends.
