## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Students often start university with "static" beliefs that mathematics is about rote procedures, which predicts lower success and higher dropout.
- **Research Problem**: To evaluate whether a redesigned first-year course can shift students' beliefs toward a "dynamic" view of mathematics (as discovery/process).
- **Key Results**: Demonstrates that explicit focus on mathematical discovery and non-routine problem solving can significantly improve students' dynamic beliefs within one semester.
- **Methodology**: Quantitative pre-post survey; N=600+.
