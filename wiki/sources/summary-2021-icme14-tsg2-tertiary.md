## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P5：理論碎片化問題
- **Research Problem**: How do tertiary mathematics instructors around the world perceive the challenges of STT, the effectiveness of current institutional measures, and the design principles required for moving forward?
- **Key Results**: A survey of 310 tertiary mathematics instructors from 30 different countries revealed vast similarities in how STT is experienced across diverse social and political contexts. Additionally, the TSG 2 agenda highlights diverse strategies to tackle STT, including flipped classrooms, commognitive reform interventions, changes in scoring practices (e.g., bullseye/circle scoring), and remediation reform (co-requisite vs. pre-requisite).
- **Methodology**: International Survey (N=310 instructors) for the main paper; accompanied by various theoretical approaches (ATD, Commognition, Morphogenesis) and mixed-method interventions reported throughout the TSG
