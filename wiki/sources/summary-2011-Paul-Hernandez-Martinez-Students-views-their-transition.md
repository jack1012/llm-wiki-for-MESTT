## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Most transition research focuses on "difficulties" and "gaps," creating a deficit-based view that ignores students' positive experiences and personal growth.
- **Research Problem**: How do students themselves perceive and narrate their transition into college-level mathematics?
- **Key Results**: Finds that students often experience the transition as a **Positive Identity Opportunity**. Instead of seeing rigor as a barrier, they see it as a chance to reinvent themselves as **Independent Learners**. Successful transition is characterized by an increase in **Student Agency**, moving from "being taught" to "taking responsibility for understanding." The study proposes that institutions should support this identity formation rather than just providing remedial content.
- **Methodology**: Qualitative longitudinal study; semi-structured interviews with students in the UK (Transmaths project).

## Structured Reading Notes
- **Theoretical Framework**: Sociocultural Identity Theory (Wenger/Holland).
- **Core Concepts**: [[Identity Growth]], [[Student Agency]], [[Self-Directed Learning]], [[Transition as Opportunity]].
- **Main Findings**: 
    - Students value the shift from "surface-level memorization" to "deep conceptual understanding" as a marker of maturity.
    - The "freedom" of college is perceived as both a challenge and a reward for academic growth.
    - Resilience is built through narratives of "accomplishment" rather than just "survival."
- **Contribution**: Provided an empirical basis for a positive, identity-focused model of transition support.

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：Hernandez-Martinez (2011) 將轉銜定義為 **「學術成年的儀式 (Rites of Academic Adulthood)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：學生對轉銜的積極看法，反映了他們對 **「專業化 (Professionalization)」** 的渴望。從「被動聽講」到「主動探究」的轉變，是從 **「學生思維」** 跨越到 **「專家思維」** 的第一步。這種 **「能動性 (Agency)」** 的養成，是所有高階職涯的核心動力。如果大學只提供「補救教學」，而不提供「身分支持」，它實際上是在延緩學生的 **「職業成熟度 (Career Maturity)」**。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未討論「負面認同」的學生如何透過社群支持重新獲得能動性。
*   **重構方向**：應在轉銜課程中明確加入 **「職業身分導引」**，讓學生看見數學能力如何轉化為未來的職涯影響力。
