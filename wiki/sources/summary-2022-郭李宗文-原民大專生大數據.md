## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Despite financial subsidies (tuition waivers), indigenous college students still suffer from a high dropout rate (22.8%), nearly double that of non-indigenous students (12.1%). The focus on economic aid ignores deeper issues of cultural and academic transition.
- **Research Problem**: Why is the current "subsidy-first" policy failing to improve retention, and why is a cross-institutional big data infrastructure necessary for indigenous education justice?
- **Key Results**: (1) **Data Fragmentation**: Vital information is scattered across the MOE, the Council of Indigenous Peoples, and university IR centers, preventing long-term tracking of student career paths. (2) **The Acculturation-Academic Rupture**: 90% of indigenous students report high academic stress, suggesting that the transition is a clash between traditional identities and Western-centric academic rigor. (3) **The "Invisible" Dropout**: Over 50% of those who suspend their studies never return, becoming an "invisible" population in professional statistics. The study advocates for a shift from "Passive Subsidies" to "Precision Guidance" powered by integrated data.

## Structured Reading Notes (中文摘要)
- **研究主題**: 台灣原住民大專生流失議題與大數據治理之倡議。
- **核心發現**:
    - **數據碎裂 (Data Fragmentation)**: 各部門間的資訊孤島導致我們無法識別學生的「跨校流動」與「離校後的職涯路徑」。
    - **文化適應壓力 (Acculturation Stress)**: 原民學生的轉銜障礙不只是錢的問題，更是進入「都市/西式大學」後的文化衝擊與學術不對稱。
    - **精準導引 (Precision Guidance)**: 提倡整合 UCAN 與 SELF-COLA 資料庫，建立原民學生的適應性追蹤系統。
- **貢獻**: 定義了「數據缺口」如何造成了原住民教育的「制度性失能」。

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：郭李宗文 (2022) 揭示了轉銜研究中的 **「數據正義與原民學生的職業生存 (Data Justice and the Professional Survival of Indigenous Students)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：原民學生的轉銜失敗，實質上是 **「身份認同與學術主體性的雙重脫軌」**。由於長期缺乏針對性的數據追蹤，這群學生在離校後往往成為 **「失落的職涯人口」**。這種數據上的缺位，本質上是一種 **「專業門檻的隱形化」**。如果我們無法看見他們在哪裡跌倒（學術、文化還是經濟），我們就無法為其建立具備 **「文化回應性 (Culturally Responsive)」** 的職涯支持路徑。數據正義即是給予這些學生「被看見」並「被精準支持」的權利。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未深入探討「原住民族學生資源中心 (原資中心)」在第一線數據蒐集與職涯輔導中的具體困境。
*   **重構方向**：應將「數據可追蹤性」標註為衡量 **「教育公平性」** 的核心指標。
