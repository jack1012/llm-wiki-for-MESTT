## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Research on the First-Year Experience (FYE) in Taiwan is characterized by "Thematic Imbalance." While there is an explosion of pedagogical research, there is a severe deficit in empirical studies on student retention and the underlying reasons for dropout.
- **Research Problem**: How has the landscape of Taiwan's FYE research evolved, and what are the critical gaps in understanding student persistence?
- **Key Results**: (1) **Technocratic Bias**: 53% of research focuses on "Teaching and Learning" (pedagogical techniques), while only 3.8% addresses "Student Retention." This suggests that institutions focus on *governing* the classroom rather than *supporting* the student's transition journey. (2) **Invisible Dropout (隱性休學)**: Identifies a large population of students who are physically present but psychologically disengaged due to a "Vacuum of Meaning." (3) **Policy-Driven Research**: Most studies are tied to government-funded projects (e.g., Higher Education Sprout Project), leading to short-term, assessment-oriented papers rather than long-term longitudinal tracking.
- **Methodology**: Bibliometric analysis using VOSviewer on 733 articles from national and international databases.

## Structured Reading Notes (中文摘要)
- **研究主題**: 台灣大一年研究之科學圖譜與演進趨勢。
- **核心發現**:
    - **議題偏食**: 台灣學界傾向研究「如何教得更好」，卻極少研究「誰正在離開」。這反映了高教管理者的技術官僚思維。
    - **隱性休學**: 定義了那些「身在校園、心在校外」的解體學生。他們的留存並非基於興趣，而是基於社會慣性或行政被動。
    - **意義感真空**: 轉銜失敗的根源在於大一課程與學生「未來職涯願景」的嚴重斷裂。
- **貢獻**: 提出了從「教學中心」轉向「學習者生命週期中心」的轉銜研究範式。

> **研究者觀點導入**：吳明錡 (2023) 揭示了轉銜教育中的 **「沈默的轉銜危機：職涯意義感的真空 (The Silent Transition Crisis: The Vacuum of Career Meaning)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：吳教授的研究揭示了台灣高教轉銜的一個悲劇：我們將學生視為 **「知識接收器」** 而非 **「生涯能動者」**。這種「技術官僚式治理」忽視了轉銜中最核心的心理動力——**「意義感」**。當學生在大一找不到所學內容與其「未來自我 (Possible Selves)」的連結時，他們就會陷入「隱性休學」。這種狀態本質上是 **「職涯社會化的中斷」**。轉銜教育的重心應從「優化教學法」轉向 **「職涯認同的建構」**。大一不應只是學分採集期，而應是 **「職業身份的初次實驗室 (The First Lab of Professional Identity)」**。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未深入探討如何在現行的「高壓升學制度」殘餘下，為大一生創造真正的探索空間。
*   **重構方向**：應將「職涯定向感」標註為衡量 **「轉銜健康度」** 的領先指標。
