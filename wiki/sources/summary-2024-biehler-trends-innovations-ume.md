## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: UME research is moving from an isolated "initial transition" focus to a broader lifecycle including professional transition and deep integration of digital technologies.
- **Research Problem**: To map emerging innovations in teaching, the evolution of transition studies, and the changing role of proof in the AI era.
- **Key Results**: Highlights three pillars: (1) Innovation in PBL/IBL/AI; (2) Multi-layered transitions (UG to PG to Employment); (3) Use of [[Proof Assistants]] (Lean) and AI (ChatGPT) for logical construction. Emphasizes [[Institutional Praxeology]] as a tool to diagnose reform failure.
- **Methodology**: Systematic survey of recent ZDM special issues and research projects (e.g., khdm project in Germany).
