## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Transition issues are traditionally viewed as purely cognitive, ignoring the "affective-sociocultural" dimension that often determines student persistence.
- **Research Problem**: To analyze the systemic issues of the STT through the lens of student expectations, emotional reactions, and institutional cultures.
- **Key Results**: Highlights the "Culture Shock" of university math; students fail not because of lack of prerequisites, but because of a mismatch in the "didactical contract" and lack of autonomous learning skills.
- **Methodology**: Qualitative analysis and survey of 1st-year math students at Italian universities.
