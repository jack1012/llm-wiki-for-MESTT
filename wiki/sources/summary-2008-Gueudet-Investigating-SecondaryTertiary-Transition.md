## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: STT research is often fragmented. Cognitive science, sociology, and didactics operate in silos, missing the integrated nature of the "total transition."
- **Research Problem**: How can we develop a comprehensive theoretical framework for understanding STT that integrates cognitive, social, and institutional perspectives?
- **Key Results**: Proposes a **Three-Dimensional Framework**: 
    1. **Cognitive**: Shift from practical to theoretical thinking (handling abstraction).
    2. **Social**: Adaptation to new sociomathematical norms (the "rules of the game").
    3. **Institutional**: Changes in the didactical contract and curricular practices.
- **Methodology**: Theoretical synthesis integrating cognitive science (Tall), sociology (Wenger), and didactics (Brousseau, Chevallard).

## Structured Reading Notes
- **Theoretical Framework**: Multi-dimensional modeling (Cognitive-Social-Institutional).
- **Core Concepts**: [[Practical vs Theoretical Thinking]], [[Sociomathematical Norms]], [[Didactical Contract]], [[Praxeology (ATD)]].
- **Main Findings**: STT is not just a "gap" but a **"Rupture"** across three levels. Cognitively, students must handle the duality of examples and formal proof. Socially, they must learn "unwritten rules" of how mathematicians talk. Institutionally, they face a "silent" contract where the teacher no longer guides every step.
- **Contribution**: Provided a systematic taxonomy that defines the modern STT research field.

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：Gueudet (2008) 的三維框架（認知、社會、制度）漏掉了關鍵的第四維度：**「生涯發展」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：Gueudet 探討了學生如何在大學「存活」，但未探討學生為何「想在大學存活」。學生的「生涯目標」（例如：為了成為數據科學家而忍受抽象代數）會調節其對認知困難與制度衝擊的忍受度。
*   **重構方向**：應將 Gueudet 的框架擴充為 **「四維度整合模型」**。生涯視角提供的「意義賦予 (Meaning-making)」是決定學生是否願意進行「社會同化 (Social Assimilation)」的核心能動性（Agency）。
