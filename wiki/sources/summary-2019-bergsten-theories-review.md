## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: STT research often essentializes cognitive dispositions, neglecting how social reproduction and institutional hierarchies shape student success or failure.
- **Research Problem**: To evaluate how post-2008 theoretical lenses (socio-cultural and sociological) add new insights to empirical STT data beyond classical categorizations.
- **Key Results**: Identifies a trend towards socio-cultural theorizing (Bourdieusian, Bernsteinian, CHAT). Highlights that a "difficult transition" can be developmentally positive, challenging the ideal of a "smooth" path.
- **Methodology**: Literature review (N=50 articles) focusing on five major theoretical frameworks from 2008-2018.
