## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P1, P2, P3：認知、制度與身分綜合
- **Research Problem**: What are the established, solid research findings regarding the causes of these STT difficulties and the promising directions for supporting novices?
- **Key Results**: The difficulties originate from higher levels of abstraction (e.g., viewing sequences as objects), implicit shifts in sociomathematical norms (e.g., changes in what constitutes a deductive proof), and deep cultural differences between secondary and tertiary institutions (e.g., doing calculations vs. learning theoretical definitions). Successful interventions include Bridging Courses, Support Centers (Tremplin), and Study and Research Paths (SRP).
- **Methodology**: Synthesis/Review article authored on behalf of the EMS Education Committee.
