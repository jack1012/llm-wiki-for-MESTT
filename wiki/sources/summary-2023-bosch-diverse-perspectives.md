## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: The "transition problem" is too often reduced to a single event (secondary to tertiary), ignoring the continuity of transitions throughout a mathematical life.
- **Research Problem**: To provide a synthesized overview of diverse theoretical perspectives on transitions, including the move to professional life.
- **Key Results**: Argues for an "Institutional" view where transitions are seen as changes in participation in different practices. Highlights the role of "Boundary Objects" between school and university.
- **Methodology**: Synthesis of research presented at the INDRUM conferences and ZDM special issues.
