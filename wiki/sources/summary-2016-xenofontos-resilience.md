## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Students from diverse cultural backgrounds often face "culture shock" in transition, but the role of "Mathematical Resilience" in mitigating this shock is not well-mapped.
- **Research Problem**: To investigate how students' resilience toward mathematical challenges (persistence, value, growth belief) influences their transition to university.
- **Key Results**: Identifies that resilience is a learned trait; students who value the struggle as part of learning adapt faster. Cultural capital influences the starting point of resilience.
- **Methodology**: Qualitative case study; interviews with multi-cultural student cohorts in the UK.
