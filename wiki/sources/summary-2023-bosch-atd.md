## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P2, P5：制度碰撞與理論框架
- **Research Problem**: How can the Anthropological Theory of the Didactic (ATD) framework explain the STT challenges and highlight the systemic response and praxeological shifts required at the university level?
- **Key Results**: ATD framework reveals systemic institutional constraints that cannot be addressed at individual level alone; Systemic Response Programs (SRP) are necessary for holistic reform; praxeological shifts in teaching and curriculum design support successful transitions.
- **Methodology**: Theoretical analysis using ATD framework; systematic review of institutional responses to STT challenges; case analysis from Spanish mathematics education context.
