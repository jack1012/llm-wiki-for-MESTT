## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Vocational and technical colleges (VTCs) in Taiwan face a double crisis: declining enrollment and high dropout rates. Unlike general universities, VTC dropouts are heavily influenced by "Professional Disillusionment" during the transition to workplace practice (internships).
- **Research Problem**: What are the deep-seated causes of dropout in VTCs, and how can Institutional Research (IR) inform more precise intervention strategies?
- **Key Results**: (1) **The Internship Barrier**: Transition failure in VTCs peaks during internship phases when students experience a "Reality Shock" between classroom ideals and workplace rigor. (2) **Premature Vocational Disillusionment**: Students often choose VTCs without clear career orientation, leading to a sudden collapse of professional identity when faced with actual labor demands. (3) **Economic-Academic Conflict**: Disadvantaged students in VTCs are more likely to prioritize immediate wage labor (part-time jobs) over long-term degree completion, viewing the degree as a "delayed" rather than "immediate" asset.
- **Methodology**: IR data mining combined with qualitative interviews of tutors and counselors.

## Structured Reading Notes (中文摘要)
- **研究主題**: 台灣技職體系學生休退學之成因分析與輔導精進策略。
- **核心發現**:
    - **實習斷層**: 技職轉銜的關鍵不在課堂而在職場。實習場域的衝擊是導致學生懷疑「職業適配性」的主因。
    - **生涯早熟型幻滅**: 學生在尚未建立足夠的職業韌性前就接觸到職場的高壓，導致專業身分認同的迅速崩解。
    - **精準輔導鏈**: 提倡從「入學前的性向引導」到「實習中的心理支持」，建立完整的職業保護鏈。
- **建議**: 應推動「彈性學習計畫」與「校內有薪實習」，以緩解弱勢生的經濟與學業衝突。

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：黃庭鍾 (2023) 揭示了技職轉銜中的核心機制：**「生涯早熟型幻滅與職業保護膜 (Premature Vocational Disillusionment and the Professional Shield)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：技職生的 STT 轉銜伴隨著極高的 **「實踐風險」**。黃教授指出的「實習屏障」，實質上是學生在尚未具備成熟職業主體性時，就直接面對了社會的專業勞動體系。這種「早熟型幻滅」會引發嚴重的 **「職涯撤資 (Vocational Divestment)」**。轉銜支持在技職體系中應被賦予 **「職業保護膜」** 的功能：在學生進入實習場域前，就應進行「職業現實校準」與「抗壓賦能」，而非單純的補救教學。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未探討「產業自動化」與「數位轉型」如何改變了技職生的實習壓力結構（例如：科技導入是否降低了體力勞動的幻滅感）。
*   **重構方向**：應將「實習適應度」標註為衡量 **「技職轉銜韌性」** 的核心指標。
