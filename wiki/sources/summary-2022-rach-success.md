## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P4：預測悖論問題
- **Research Problem**: What individual (prior knowledge, learning strategies, epistemological beliefs) and contextual factors (institutional support, teaching quality) distinguish students who achieve success and subjective satisfaction in the STT?
- **Key Results**: Individual factors (prior knowledge, learning strategies) and contextual factors (institutional support, teaching quality) are equally important for both academic success and subjective satisfaction; satisfaction is not merely derivative of academic success; affective and cognitive dimensions of success are partially decoupled.
- **Methodology**: Mixed-methods study combining quantitative prediction models with qualitative interviews; measurement of success (grades) and satisfaction (subjective well-being); analysis of individual and contextual predictors; multilevel modeling.
