## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: The rapid decline in birth rates has created a survival crisis for many higher education institutions, particularly private vocational and technical colleges.
- **Research Problem**: What is the current distribution of freshman registration rates across Taiwan, and which types of institutions are most vulnerable to the demographic cliff?
- **Key Results**: (1) **The Polarization Effect**: Public universities maintain high registration rates (near 100%), while many private institutions, especially those in rural areas, fall below the 60% "Drowning Line." (2) **Vocational Vulnerability**: Private vocational colleges (技專校院) exhibit the most volatile registration patterns, reflecting a declining social prestige and an unstable career outlook for their graduates. (3) **The "Ghost Department" Phenomenon**: Identifying departments with zero enrollment, signaling a massive mismatch between industrial demand and academic supply.
- **Methodology**: Quantitative census of all higher education enrollment data for the 106 academic year.

## Structured Reading Notes (中文摘要)
- **研究主題**: 106 學年度全台大專校院新生入學註冊率之現狀分析。
- **核心數據**:
    - **註冊率斷層**: 全台有 17 所大學註冊率低於 60%，面臨退場紅線。
    - **生源分流**: 學生與家長對「公立大學」與「指標型校系」的集體向心力，導致私立與非指標校系的生源被極端壓縮。
- **貢獻**: 定義了台灣高教轉銜中的「市場選擇壓力」，迫使大學重新思考其品牌價值與轉銜支持。

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：教育部 (2018a) 揭示了轉銜中的 **「預防性棄船行為 (Preventive Abandonment Behavior)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：新生註冊率不僅是行政指標，更是家長與學生對未來職涯的 **「集體投票」**。低註冊率背後是學生對該校系提供的 **「職涯承諾」** 投下不信任票。這種「預防性棄船」導致了轉銜中的 **「負向循環」**：低註冊率導致資源匱乏，資源匱乏進一步導致教學品質下降，最終促使大一生在入學第一學期就積極準備「二次轉銜（轉學考）」。這種心態讓大一不再是專業起點，而變成了 **「備戰中繼站」**。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未深入探討註冊率與該校畢業生實際起薪的相關性。
*   **重構方向**：應將「註冊率」視為衡量 **「系所品牌與職場聯通性」** 的信號指標。
