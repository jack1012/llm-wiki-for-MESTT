## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P7, P1：專業任務的介入設計與認知吸引
- **Research Problem**: How do professional task-related elements enhance student motivation and interest in mathematics-oriented programmes, and how do these factors influence the attractiveness of transitioning from school to university mathematics?
- **Key Results**: Professional task-related elements in mathematics curricula significantly enhance student motivation and interest in transitioning to mathematics-oriented programmes; task design that connects to real-world applications increases perceived relevance; affective engagement with professional contexts mediates the cognitive challenges of formal mathematics.
- **Methodology**: Mixed-methods study; design-based research on professional task integration; measurement of student interest and motivation; analysis of task design principles that support engagement.
