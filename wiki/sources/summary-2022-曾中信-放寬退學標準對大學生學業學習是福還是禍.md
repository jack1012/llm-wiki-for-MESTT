## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Taiwanese universities are increasingly relaxing academic dismissal policies (from "Double 2/1" to "Consecutive 2/1" or abolition) to protect student rights. However, this may inadvertently lead to a **"Moral Hazard"** where students reduce their learning effort.
- **Research Problem**: Does the relaxation of dismissal standards improve student welfare or lead to a decline in academic performance, especially for at-risk students?
- **Key Results**: (1) **Systemic Decline**: Relaxing standards leads to a significant increase in course failure rates and a decrease in weighted average scores (Z-scores). (2) **Disproportionate Impact**: Low-achieving students are the most severely affected, with their failure probability soaring by 54%. (3) **Moral Hazard**: Students adopt strategic behaviors (e.g., withdrawing from difficult courses) to survive administratively without improving their actual competence. The study concludes that the "Academic Dismissal Policy" serves as a critical **Incentive Mechanism** for maintaining professional standards.
- **Methodology**: Difference-in-Differences (DID) with Fixed Effects; Sample N=10,146 students across multiple cohorts.

## Structured Reading Notes (中文摘要)
- **研究主題**: 台灣大專院校退學標準放寬對學生學業表現之影響。
- **核心發現**:
    - **道德風險 (Moral Hazard)**: 當學生發現即便表現不佳也不會被退學時，其投入學習的動能顯著下降。
    - **中後段學生的重災區**: 政策本意是保護弱勢學生，但數據顯示，正是這群人因標準放寬而產生的學業下滑最為嚴重，形成了 **「溫柔的排擠」**。
    - **制度警惕作用**: 「雙二一」制具備強大的預警與警惕功能，而「連續二一」則極易被學生透過策略性選課規避。
- **貢獻**: 證實了退學標準是維護高教品質與學生職業韌性的最後一道防線。

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：曾中信 (2022) 揭示了轉銜期中 **「虛假安全感的職涯陷阱 (The Trap of False Security)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：放寬退學標準雖然增加了學生的「行政安全感」，卻在無形中稀釋了其 **「生涯資本 (Career Capital)」**。在轉銜的關鍵期（大一、大二），如果制度不再要求學生的努力程度，學生會產生一種「我不努力也能生存」的錯覺。這種 **「職涯適應性的遲緩」** 會在畢業進入職場時爆發為劇烈的挫折。這篇文章提醒我們：轉銜支持應是 **「能力賦能」** 而非 **「標準放寬」**。對學生的真正仁慈，是維持其具備職業競爭力的嚴謹性。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未探討那些因政策受惠而留在學校的學生，最終是否能成功畢業並進入預期職涯。
*   **重構方向**：應將「及格率」視為衡量 **「職業門檻跨越能力」** 的先行指標。
