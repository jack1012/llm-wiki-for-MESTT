## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P4, P1：預測悖論與認知調適
- **Research Problem**: How do self-regulated learning (SRL) strategies relate to and predict mathematical achievement in the STT, and what metacognitive practices support student success at the university level?
- **Key Results**: Self-regulated learning strategies significantly predict mathematical achievement in STT; metacognitive awareness and strategic monitoring correlate with success; SRL-aware instruction improves retention and conceptual understanding.
- **Methodology**: Quantitative correlational study; survey of self-regulated learning strategies with regression analysis of achievement outcomes; data from undergraduate mathematics students.
