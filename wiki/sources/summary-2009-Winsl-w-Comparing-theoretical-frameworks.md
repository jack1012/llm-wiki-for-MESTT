## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: The field of mathematics education is saturated with diverse, often isolated theoretical frameworks (ATD, TDS, CoP, etc.), making it difficult to synthesize findings on complex phenomena like the STT.
- **Research Problem**: How can we create a meta-framework to systematically compare and integrate different didactic theories?
- **Key Results**: Proposes the **GOA-model**, identifying three fundamental components of any didactic system: **G (Groups/Humans)** (the actors), **O (Organizations)** (the mathematical structures/praxeologies), and **A (Artefacts)** (the tools/language). The study finds that theories differ primarily in their **"Ontological Foreground"**—which component they treat as the primary lens. In the STT context, Winsløw argues that success requires "Networking" these theories: using ATD to analyze the O-gap (content) and CoP to analyze the G-gap (identity).
- **Methodology**: Theoretical meta-modeling and comparative analysis of European didactic frameworks.

## Structured Reading Notes

- **Main Findings**: 
    - No single theory can model the "Total Social Fact" of the transition; they are complementary rather than contradictory.
    - Transition is an **"Epistemic Rupture"** across all three GOA axes: different groups (G), different praxeologies (O), and different symbolic tools (A).
- **Contribution**: Provided the foundational logic for multi-theoretical research in the transition field.

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：Winsløw (2009) 揭示了轉銜研究中的 **「結構化洞察力與職涯系統思維 (Structured Insight and Career Systems Thinking)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：Winsløw 透過 GOA 模型告訴我們，成功的專業人士必須具備 **「元分析能力 (Meta-analytical Capability)」**。在高階職涯（如系統架構、政策分析、大型專案管理）中，個體經常面臨「理論叢林」與衝突的資訊框架。GOA 模型實際上是在訓練學生的 **「系統化視角」**：不被單一的「教學法」或「技術工具」困住，而是能同時看透人 (G)、組織/知識架構 (O) 與工具環境 (A) 之間的互動。轉銜失敗的學生，往往是因為他們只在單一軸度（如純技術 A）上努力，而忽略了組織 (O) 與群體身份 (G) 的深刻變革。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未討論如何在大一課程中顯性地介紹這種「元思維」以降低學生的認知負荷。
*   **重構方向**：應將「元模型分析能力」標註為衡量 **「高階專家主體性」** 的核心指標。
