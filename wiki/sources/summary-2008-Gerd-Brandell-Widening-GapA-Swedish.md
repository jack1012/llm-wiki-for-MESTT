## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: The transition in Sweden has shifted from an elite to a mass system, resulting in a **"Widening Gap"** where secondary school graduates lack the fundamental skills in deductive reasoning required for university mathematics.
- **Research Problem**: What are the specific components of this gap, and how do they impact student persistence in STEM fields?
- **Key Results**: Identifies a critical **"Rigor Deficit"**. While school curricula focus on "breadth" and "calculator-based problem solving," they have sacrificed **Deductive Reasoning and Proof**. The study uses the metaphor of **"Bricks without Cement"**: students arrive with mathematical facts (bricks) but lack the logical structure (cement) to build a coherent system. This lead to a **Structural Mismatch** where university courses must be "remedialized," slowing down the professionalization of future engineers and scientists.
- **Methodology**: Longitudinal curriculum analysis combined with surveys of students (N=170) and faculty at KTH and Stockholm University.

## Structured Reading Notes
- **Theoretical Framework**: Trow's Theory of Mass Higher Education; Tall's Cognitive Discontinuity.
- **Core Concepts**: [[Curriculum Gap]], [[Mass Education Paradox]], [[Proof as Cement]], [[The Procedural-Formal Rupture]].
- **Main Findings**: 
    - The Swedish school reform prioritized "motivation" and "relevance" over "abstract rigor."
    - University faculty perceive a sharp decline in students' ability to handle **Symbolic Algebra** and **Logical Derivation**.
    - Students feel a sense of **"Educational Betrayal"** when their high school success fails to translate into university performance.
- **Contribution**: Provided the first major empirical documentation of the "Swedish Mathematics Crisis."

## Career Development Analysis (生涯發展觀點)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **研究者觀點導入**：Brandell (2008) 揭示了 **「大眾化教育下的職涯資格焦慮 (Vocational Credential Anxiety in Mass Education)」**。

*   **生涯層次的體現**：隨著瑞典大學入學門檻的放寬，越來越多追求 **「職涯社會流動」** 而非「純粹學術熱情」的學生進入 STEM 領域。這類學生的 **「職涯動機」** 主要是外部導向的。當他們遇到「擴大的落差」時，感到的不是學術挑戰，而是對其 **「未來職涯資格 (Professional Credentials)」** 的威脅。這種焦慮導致他們傾向於「機械式記憶」以求生存，而非真正的「深層學習」。這反映了 **「職涯穩定性」與「專業嚴謹性」** 之間的內在衝突。
*   **文獻忽略之處**：未討論「產學合作」如何能作為緩解「課程落差」的替代性學習路徑。
*   **重構方向**：應將「演繹推理能力」標註為衡量學生 **「職業決策品質」** 的核心指標。
