## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Pedagogical practices in the first year often undermine students' mathematical identities by reinforcing a sense of alienation and lack of agency.
- **Research Problem**: To explore how sociocultural contexts and institutional practices shape students' mathematical identities during the transition to university.
- **Key Results**: Identifies the importance of "leading identity" in determining student engagement. Mismatch between institutional expectations and student motives leads to "Identity Tension".
- **Methodology**: Qualitative case study; longitudinal interviews and classroom observations.
