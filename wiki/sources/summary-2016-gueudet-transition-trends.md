## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Traditional STT research is often siloed by local institutional norms, lacking a unified theoretical dialogue for global trends.
- **Research Problem**: To map the global transition research landscape from 2000-2016, identifying the shift from cognitive adjustment to regime change.
- **Key Results**: Identifies three research avenues: (1) Cognitive conceptual change; (2) Sociocultural practices; (3) Institutional design (SRP). Marks the rise of theory networking as the necessary evolution of the field.
- **Methodology**: ICME-13 Topical Survey and systematic review of international educational databases.
