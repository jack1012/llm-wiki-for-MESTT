## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: P1：認知斷裂問題
- **Research Problem**: What cognitive difficulties arise when students transition from using operable definitions to understanding and proving theorems formally at university?
- **Key Results**: Students frequently fail to transition from using operable definitions (procedural, image-based) to formal, proved theorems; the cognitive conflict between proof-by-example and formal proof is a critical barrier in university mathematics.
- **Methodology**: Qualitative case study analysis; analysis of mathematical concept development through Tall's three-world framework (enactive, iconic, symbolic).
