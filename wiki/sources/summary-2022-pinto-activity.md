## Core Claims (English Only)
- **Problematic**: Tertiary mathematics requires students to engage in "advanced mathematical activity" (defining, conjecturing, proving) that differs fundamentally from secondary algorithmic procedures, necessitating explicit pedagogical transition support.
- **Research Problem**: To define and characterize "advanced mathematical activity" appropriate for tertiary mathematics transition, examining how task design and pedagogical framing can support students' development of proof and argumentation practices essential for university mathematics.
- **Key Results**: ## 關鍵發見 (Key Findings)
- **Methodology**: Task design research; qualitative analysis of mathematical activity; INDRUM conference proceedings; defining-conjecturing-proving activity framework.
